# Chemistry Bench Area

The chemistry and reagent preparation area in Robbins 170. Contains the [[analytical-balance]], [[top-loading-balance]], [[ph-meter]], [[stir-plate]], and is adjacent to the dry chemical storage, [[cabinet-chemical]], [[cabinet-flammable]], and [[cabinet-corrosive]].

Buffer preparation, weighing, pH adjustment, and solution making all happen here.
