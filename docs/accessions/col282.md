# COL282

*Manihot esculenta* (cultivated cassava) sequenced for [[Kehan Zhao]]'s 2026 *New Phytologist* paper on Colombian cassava landrace genomic diversity. Colombian landrace from the CIAT germplasm collection.

One of nine accessions selected for full PacBio HiFi de novo assembly in addition to short-read whole-genome sequencing.
