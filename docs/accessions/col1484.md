# COL1484

*Manihot esculenta* (cultivated cassava) sequenced for [[Kehan Zhao]]'s 2026 *New Phytologist* paper on Colombian cassava landrace genomic diversity. Colombian landrace from the CIAT germplasm collection.

This is a **HiFi-only** accession — selected for full PacBio HiFi de novo assembly but not included in the Illumina WGS batches. One of nine HiFi-assembly accessions for the Zhao et al. 2026 paper.
