# PER413-5A

*Manihot esculenta* subsp. *peruviana* (a wild progenitor of cassava, native to Peru) sequenced for [[Kehan Zhao]]'s 2026 *New Phytologist* paper on Colombian cassava landrace genomic diversity. Peruvian *M. esculenta* subsp. *peruviana* accession.

One of nine accessions selected for full PacBio HiFi de novo assembly in addition to short-read whole-genome sequencing.
