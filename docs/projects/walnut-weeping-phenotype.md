# Walnut Weeping Phenotype

Short-read WGS of two *Juglans regia* trees displaying a weeping growth phenotype. Part of [[Matt Davis]]'s 2026-03-28 walnut sample-collection trip, alongside [[walnut-vx211|VX211 off-types]] and [[walnut-cut-leaf|cut leaf]]. Provenance traced via Chuck Leslie's archival work.

## Summary

The weeping phenotype in walnut is a heritable architectural variant. Whether the cases sampled here are clonal somatic events, fixed germline variants, or allelic states from an existing breeding line is the open question. Two trees were sampled (`R1_Weep`, `R2_Weep`); both are queued for Illumina WGS once the [[kerman-somatic|Kerman Somatic]] pipeline finishes.

This is the lowest-priority of Matt's three new walnut sample sets ([[walnut-vx211|VX211]] → [[walnut-cut-leaf|Cut leaf]] → Weeping). Tissue is stored in the -80 freezer.
