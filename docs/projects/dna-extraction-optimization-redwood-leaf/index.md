# DNA Extraction Optimization - Redwood Leaf

## Summary

**Objective:** Optimize Qiagen DNeasy Plant Mini Kit protocol for Sequoia sempervirens (coast redwood) leaf tissue. Compare standard elution (100uL AE buffer) vs. reduced volume (50uL AE buffer) for higher concentration yields.

## Team

* James Freckles (lead)
* Grey Monroe (PI)

## Protocols

* Tissue grinding (mechanical disruption in liquid nitrogen)
* DNeasy Plant Mini Kit - standard (100uL elution)
* DNeasy Plant Mini Kit - modified (50uL elution)

## Status

Extraction complete. QC passed. DNA stored at -80C.
