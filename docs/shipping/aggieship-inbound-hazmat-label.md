# AggieShip Inbound Hazmat Label (Lab Side)

> **Status: pending.** This protocol covers the lab-side workflow for generating a prepaid AggieShip Dry Ice / Cat B / Excepted Quantities shipping label and emailing it to an off-campus collaborator who will ship frozen biological samples to the Monroe Lab. It will be written once we've completed an authenticated Playwright walkthrough of the AggieShip Dry Ice wizard with real screenshots, including verification of the Ship From `OTHER` lookup, the Package Contains dropdown options, and real cost numbers from MD/OR to Davis.

## What this will cover (when it's written)

- **Prerequisites**: AggieShip Hazardous Materials Shipping Training cert (UC Learning Center), submission to `aggieship@ucdavis.edu`, default account setup
- **Collecting info from the collaborator**: number of tubes, gross package weight (kg), box dimensions, dry ice weight (kg), full ship-from address, intended ship date (Mon/Tue/Wed)
- **Creating the label** in AggieShip's "Create Dry Ice, Category B, Excepted Quantities Shipment" wizard, click by click with screenshots
- **Selecting the right Package Contains classification** for transgenic vs. wild-type plant samples (pending GMO/GMMO ruling from `aggieship@ucdavis.edu` + `hazshipping@ucdavis.edu`)
- **Sending the PDF to the collaborator** with packing instructions
- **Re-printing or cancelling** an issued label
- **What to do when something goes wrong** (review delays, weight mismatches, classification disputes)
