### Detailed Experimental Notes

**Kits/Reagents Used:**

- Sterilization solution: sodium hypochlorite (~6% household bleach), 20% Triton X-100, sterile dH2O
- Edwards buffer for quick DNA extraction (200 mM Tris pH 7.5, 250 mM NaCl, 25 mM EDTA, 0.5% SDS)

**Solutions Made or Used:**

- Sterilization solution (fresh): 1 mL bleach + 50 uL Triton X-100 + dH2O to 10 mL
- Edwards buffer (used existing stock from fridge)

**Biological Samples:**

- Col-0 wild type (~50 seeds, plated)
- atx12r7 (~60 seeds, sterilized and stratifying)
- msh6 (~60 seeds, sterilized and stratifying)
- pds5a-e (~60 seeds, sterilized and stratifying)
- Col-0 3-week leaf tissue (practice extraction, from Kehan's plants)

**Procedure & Deviations:**

**Seed plating (Col-0):** Used a pipette tip to space seeds on MS plates, ~12 seeds per plate across 4 plates. Sealed with micropore tape. Placed in growth chamber at 22C, 16h light.

**Seed sterilization (mutant lines):** Used Method 2 (bleach/Triton) from the [seed sterilization protocol](../../wet-lab/seed-sterilization.md) since Kehan said these seed stocks are from 2024 and might have more contamination. First wash 5 min, second wash 15 min, 3x rinse with sterile water. Seeds looked fine, no bleaching.

!!! variant "Deviation: Extended first wash"
    Extended the first wash to 7 min instead of 5 min for the atx12r7 seeds because the tube had visible debris floating. Seeds still looked healthy after.

**Stratification:** Resuspended all three lines in 1 mL sterile water, wrapped in foil, placed in 4C cold room. Will plate Thursday (2 days).

**Quick DNA extraction (practice):** Followed the [quick extraction protocol](../../wet-lab/quick-dna-extraction.md) with ~2 leaves of Col-0. Ground with a pestle in Edwards buffer, incubated 10 min, centrifuged, took supernatant, isopropanol precipitation. Got a visible pellet.
