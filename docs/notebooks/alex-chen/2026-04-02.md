### Detailed Experimental Notes

**Kits/Reagents Used:**

- GoTaq Green Master Mix (Promega)
- atx12r7 genotyping primers (LP, RP, T-DNA LB from Kehan's primer box, row C)
- 1% agarose gel, 1x TAE buffer
- GelRed stain

**Biological Samples:**

- Col-0 gDNA from practice extraction (AC-ext-001, 85 ng/uL)
- atx12r7, msh6, pds5a-e stratified seeds (from 4C cold room)

**Procedure & Deviations:**

**Plating mutant seeds:** Removed tubes from 4C, pipetted seeds onto MS plates in the clean bench. ~15 seeds per plate, 4 plates per genotype (12 plates total). Sealed with micropore tape, placed in growth chamber next to Col-0 plates.

**PCR genotyping (practice):**

Followed [PCR genotyping protocol](../../wet-lab/pcr-genotyping.md). Set up two reactions for Col-0 template:

1. LP + RP (wild-type band, expected ~1100 bp)
2. LB + RP (T-DNA band, should be absent in Col-0)

| Component | Volume |
|-----------|--------|
| GoTaq Green 2x | 10 uL |
| Forward primer (10 uM) | 1 uL |
| Reverse primer (10 uM) | 1 uL |
| Template DNA (~50 ng) | 1 uL |
| Water | 7 uL |
| **Total** | **20 uL** |

PCR program: 95C 3 min, then 35 cycles of (95C 30s, 56C 30s, 72C 1 min), 72C 5 min, hold 4C.

**Gel electrophoresis:** Ran 1% agarose gel at 120V for 35 min. Loaded 5 uL of each reaction + 2 uL loading dye. 1 kb ladder in lane 1.
