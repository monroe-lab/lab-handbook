3. Useful resources for lab safety

- [Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/18Sbi_aPXZA9c26VztOXxbyJsYmdF4K21?usp=drive_link) provide general safety guidance for lab work and keep a record of your safety training, they also tell you what hazardous chemicals we have in our lab

- [Chemical Inventory](https://ehs.ucop.edu/chemicals/) is a chemical inventory for our lab, where you can search for specific chemicals and see location and safety information. Chemical Storage rules:

- All corrosives need secondary containment.
- Reproductive hazards and carcinogens need to be in sealed containers.
- Acids/bases need to be stored in secondary containment. Flammables do not, as far as I am aware.
- All chemicals within the secondary containment need to be compatible. For example, acetic acid and nitric acid should not be in the same secondary containment or cabinet. Hydrochloric acid and acetic acid should not be in the same secondary containment, but can be in the same cabinet. Or acetic acid can be in the flammable cabinet as long as it has its own secondary container.

- [Safety Data Sheets](https://safetyservices.ucdavis.edu/units/ehs/research/safety-data-sheets) provide information on the hazards of working with a chemical and procedures that should be used to ensure safety

- How to deal with hazardous waste? Use [WASTe](https://safetyservices.ucdavis.edu/units/ehs/hazardous-materials-management/waste-tracking).

- Login with your UCD credentials, click on “Create a New Tag” on the right, choose either “Chemical” or “Universal”, fill in the waste infomation (Because Robbins 0170 is a large room including other labs, better to specify the detailed location, for example, the hume hood is located in Bay 0170U).
- Print the tag, tape the tag on the waste container
- Go to WASTe website again, click on “View My Tags”, and click on the arrow symbol next to “Tracking #” to be ready for pickup
- Hazardous waste must be closed, double-bagged, in a secondary container, and NOT on the floor
