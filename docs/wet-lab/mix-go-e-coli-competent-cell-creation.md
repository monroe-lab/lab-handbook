# Mix & Go! E. coli Competent Cell Creation

Reference: [Zymo Research Protocol](https://files.zymoresearch.com/protocols/_t3001_t3002_mix_go_e._coli_transformation_kit_buffer_set.pdf)

1. Sterilize 500mL culture flask by autoclaving
2. In sterilized 500mL flask, inoculate 50mL SOB (ZymoBroth) with 0.5mL fresh, overnight E. coli culture (grown in LB)
3. Loosely cover 500mL flask with foil and place on shaking incubator (175rpm at 26°C) until OD 0.4-0.6 (\~6-8 hours)
4. On ice, prepare 1X Buffers by combining 1 part of wash/competent buffer with 1 part dilution buffers
    1. 2.5mL 2X Wash Buffer and 2.5mL Dilution Buffer
    2. 2.5mL 2X Competent Buffer and 2.5mL Dilution Buffer
5. Transfer Stellar E. coli from culture flask to 50mL centrifuge tube and place on ice for 10 minutes
6. Centrifuge at 3,500rpm for 10 minutes at 4°C (place another centrifuge tube with 50mL of water for balance)
7. Remove the supernatant and resuspend the cells gently in 5 mL ice-cold 1X Wash Buffer
8. Re-pellet the cells as in Step 8 but with 5mL of water for balance
9. Completely remove the supernatant and gently resuspended the cells in 5 mL ice-cold 1X Competent Buffer
10. Aliquot (on ice) 0.2 ml of the cell suspension into sterile microcentrifuge tubes
11. Label and place tubes in -80C
