## Overview

Qiagen DNeasy Plant Mini Kit protocol for genomic DNA isolation from plant tissue. Based on Qiagen handbook with modifications for Sequoia sempervirens.

## Kit Contents

* DNeasy Mini Spin Columns (silica membrane)
* Collection tubes (2mL)
* Buffer AP1 (lysis buffer)
* Buffer AP2 (precipitation buffer)
* Buffer AP3/E (binding buffer, add ethanol before first use)
* Buffer AW (wash buffer, add ethanol before first use)
* Buffer AE (elution buffer: 10mM Tris-Cl, 0.5mM EDTA, pH 9.0)
* RNase A (100mg/mL stock)

## Additional Materials Required

* Ethanol (96-100%) for buffer preparation
* Microcentrifuge capable of 14,000 rpm
* Water bath or heating block set to 65C
* 1.5mL microcentrifuge tubes
* Pipettes and filter tips (200uL, 1000uL)

## Procedure

1. Grind tissue to fine powder in liquid nitrogen (see tissue grinding protocol).
2. Add 400uL Buffer AP1 and 4uL RNase A stock solution to the tube containing ground tissue. Vortex vigorously.
3. Incubate the mixture at 65C for 10 minutes. Invert tube 2-3 times during incubation to mix the lysate.
4. Add 130uL Buffer AP2 to the lysate. Mix thoroughly and incubate on ice for 5 minutes.
5. Centrifuge the lysate at 14,000 rpm for 5 minutes.
6. Transfer the supernatant (including any precipitate) to a QIAshredder Mini Spin Column. Centrifuge for 2 minutes at 14,000 rpm.
7. Transfer the flow-through to a new tube without disturbing the pellet. Add 1.5 volumes of Buffer AP3/E. Mix by pipetting.
8. Transfer 650uL of the mixture to a DNeasy Mini Spin Column placed in a 2mL collection tube. Centrifuge for 1 minute at 8,000 rpm. Discard flow-through. Repeat with remaining mixture.
9. Add 500uL Buffer AW to the spin column. Centrifuge for 1 minute at 8,000 rpm. Discard flow-through.
10. Add 500uL Buffer AW to the spin column. Centrifuge for 2 minutes at 14,000 rpm to dry the membrane.
11. Transfer the spin column to a new 1.5mL microcentrifuge tube. Add 100uL Buffer AE directly to the membrane. Incubate for 5 minutes at room temperature.
12. Centrifuge for 1 minute at 8,000 rpm to elute the DNA.

## Expected Results

* Yield: 2-30ug genomic DNA per 100mg leaf tissue
* Elution volume: 100uL in Buffer AE
* 260/280 ratio: 1.7-2.0 (pure DNA)
* 260/230 ratio: >2.0 (no contaminant carryover)
