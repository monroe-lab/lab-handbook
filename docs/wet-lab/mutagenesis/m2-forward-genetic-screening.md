2024 Oct Soya

[protocol] M2 Fwd genetic screening

### Timeline

### Seed sterilization (Tuesday)

Materials

- Sample seeds. Which are usually..

- 18 dry-cleaned, aliquoted seeds in 5 ml tubes (Cleaned by Li, Vianney and Jiani)
- WT and atx12r7 seeds. Approx 30 seeds per each

- Sterilized water (500 ml)
- Sterilization Solution (200 ml), prepare on the day

| Items | Amount to add | location |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Bleach | 25 ml | Under the sink |
| 20 % Triton | 1 ml | Soya’s bench |
| DI water | Vol up to 200 ml | Tap, with white lever |
- Two Tube racks (soya’s bench or nearby)
- Empty beaker (to collect waste liquid)
- automatic pipet (near the scales) and 25 ml pipet tip (at the bottom drawer under scales)

Procedure

- Collect the week’s sample seeds and place them on tube racks
- If there are seeds sticking to the top part of the tube, tap or [[centrifuge]] to let them fall to the bottom
- Using pipet, add approx. 5 ml of sterilization solution to each tube. Careful not to overflow
- Place the tubes on rotator, rotate for 10 min
- Centrifuge at 500 g for a few seconds to pellet the seeds
- Pour out the sterilization solution. Careful not to pour out the seeds.
- Using pipet, add approx. 5 ml of sterilization solution to each tube. Careful not to overflow
- Place the tubes on rotator, rotate for 10 min
- Centrifuge at 500 g for a few seconds to pellet the seeds
- Take the tubes & racks to the [[clean-bench]][1]
- Pour out the sterilization solution. Careful not to pour out the seeds.

- Pour in sterilized water. Careful not to overflow. Close the lid and mix (1st rinse)
- Centrifuge at 500 g for a few seconds to pellet the seeds
- Pour out the water. Careful not to pour out the seeds.
- Pour in sterilized water. Careful not to overflow. Close the lid and mix (2nd rinse)
- Centrifuge at 500 g for a few seconds to pellet the seeds
- Pour out the water. Careful not to pour out the seeds.
- Fill the tube with sterilized water. Put the tubes in a plastic bag, wrap in foil and put in the fridge

### Seed plating (Thursday)

Materials

- X-ray irradiated seeds
- Tube racks
- MS plates
- 2 ml serological pipet tip (1 + the number of the samples)
- A few inoculation loop
- White plastic sheet that comes with surgical tape
- Surgical tapes

Procedure

- Centrifuge the tubes at 500 g for a few seconds to pellet the seeds
- Place everything in the clean bench[1]
- Label the plates.

- Take one plate, draw a line on the bottom plate to divide into two sections. Using sharpie, label sections as WT and atx12r7.
- Sample tubes have a three-digit number on it. Using sharpie, label the bottom corner of plates with the number, at default two plates per tube. If the tube contains a small number of seeds, label only one plate.

- Pour the water out of the tube, careful not to lose the seeds
- To the WT/atx12r7 tubes…

- Add only a few drops of 0.3 % Agar.
- Using 1 ml pipet, resuspend the seeds in the agar
- Take the seed solution into a sterilized 1 ml tip. Touch the surface of the MS plate with the tip to plant the seed. If you resuspend the seeds in the right concentration and touch with the right strength, one touch releases one seed.

- To the other tubes…

- Using a manual pipet with a 2 ml tip, add 4 ml of 0.3 % Agar to the tube. If the tube contains a small number of seeds, add only 2 ml. Pipet up and down to resuspend the seeds.
- Drop the seed resuspension to the plate, 2 ml/ plates. The resuspension is viscous. drop slowly to eject all the seeds!
- Using an inoculation loop, evenly distribute the seeds over the surface. Using a white sheet as a background is helpful.

- Seal all the plates with surgical tape
- Scan the plates in the scanner in the lab

- Log in to the  PC’s with the password pl@ntsRfr1ends
- Open epson scan2
- Set the name prefix as Fwdgenetics\_[the smallest number on the tube]\_[largest number of the tube]
- Set dpi to 600
- Place two plates bottom down on the scanner, a few inches away from the edge. Layer the white background screen on top, and hit ‘preview’
- Set the trimming window to select the plate areas
- Scan. Continue for all the plates.

- Place the plates in the incubator, face up, avoiding overlap.

[1] How to Use the Clean Bench

- Generously coat the surfaces in the clean bench with 70% EtOH.
- Switch it on top. When you turn it on, filtered, clean air flow down from the upper part of the clean bench and fill the bench space with clean air.
- Generously coat the surface of EVERYTHING you put into the clean bench with 70% EtOH, including your hands and arms.
- When you are done, switch OFF.
