## Location

The growth chambers are in **[[room-asmundson-growth-chamber|Asmussen Hall]]**, not in [[room-robbins-0170|Robbins 170]]. Your mentor or Grey will show you the location and building access on your first visit.

## Standard Arabidopsis Conditions

| Parameter | Setting |
|-----------|---------|
| Temperature | 22C (day and night) |
| Light cycle | 16 hours light / 8 hours dark (long day) |
| Light intensity | ~100-150 umol/m2/s (standard fluorescent or LED) |
| Humidity | Ambient (no active humidity control in most chambers) |

These are the default conditions for Col-0 and most Arabidopsis ecotypes. Some experiments require different conditions (short day, cold treatment, etc.). Grey will specify if your experiment needs something different.

## Procedure

### Setting temperature

1. Locate the temperature controller (digital display on the front of the chamber).
2. The set point should read **22C**. If it does not, ask Grey before changing it (other people's experiments may be in the chamber).
3. Verify the actual temperature matches the set point. If the chamber is far off (more than 2C), report it.

### Setting the light cycle

1. Growth chambers typically have a programmable timer for lights.
2. Standard setting: lights ON at 6:00 AM, lights OFF at 10:00 PM (16h light / 8h dark).
3. Do not change the light cycle without checking with Grey. Other experiments in the same chamber depend on the schedule.

### Placing your plants

1. Label your tray or flat clearly with your name, date, genotype, and experiment.
2. Place plates or pots on a shelf with good light exposure. Avoid the edges of shelves where light intensity drops.
3. For plates (MS media with seedlings): tape plates shut with [[micropore-tape]], stand them upright or at a slight angle so roots grow downward and shoots grow upward.
4. For pots: place in a tray to catch water runoff.

### Monitoring

- Check your plants every 1-2 days during active growth periods.
- Look for: germination, growth rate, contamination on plates, wilting, pest damage.
- See [[watering-and-plant-care]] for watering schedule and troubleshooting.

## Developmental Timeline (Col-0, 22C, long day)

| Days after planting | Stage |
|--------------------|-------|
| 2-3 | Radicle emergence (root appears) |
| 5-7 | Cotyledons (seed leaves) open |
| 10-14 | First true leaves visible |
| 21-28 | Rosette established, multiple true leaves |
| 28-35 | Bolting (inflorescence stem elongates) |
| 35-42 | Flowering |
| 56-70 | Siliques (seed pods) maturing |
| 70-84 | Seeds mature, plant begins to dry |

These are approximate. Growth rate varies by ecotype, mutant genotype, and exact conditions.

## Documentation

Create a lab notebook entry when you set up plants in the chamber. Date it. Note: chamber number/location, conditions (temp, light cycle), what you planted, shelf position. Take a photo of your setup.
