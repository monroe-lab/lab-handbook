# Make 0.1% Agar Seed Suspension

## Resources

**Equipment:** [[top-loading-balance]], [[autoclave-sterilizer]], bottle with loosened cap

**Reagents:** [[agar-non-tc|Agar]] (any grade), distilled water

**Related Protocols:** [[how-to-use-the-scale]], [[autoclave]], [[planting-arabidopsis-on-ms-plates]]

**Prerequisites:** [[how-to-use-the-scale]], [[autoclave]]

**Purpose:** Make a dilute agar solution used to suspend Arabidopsis seeds for plating. The slight viscosity of 0.1% agar keeps tiny seeds in suspension so you can pipette them onto plates one at a time instead of trying to pick them up with forceps.

## Time estimate

**Wall time:** ~1 hr (mostly autoclave) | **Hands-on:** ~10 min

---

## Before You Start

Check that you have:

- Agar powder (any type; this is not tissue culture grade, just structural)
- A clean autoclavable bottle

## Background

Arabidopsis seeds are tiny (~250 um) and static-prone. Dry seeds cling to surfaces and are difficult to handle individually. Resuspending them in 0.1% agar creates a slightly viscous liquid that:

- Keeps seeds from sinking to the bottom immediately
- Allows you to pipette individual seeds with a P1000 or P200
- Does not interfere with germination

## Recipe: 100 mL of 0.1% Agar

| Component | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Agar powder | 0.1 g |
| Distilled water | 100 mL |

## Procedure

1. Weigh **0.1 g [[agar-non-tc|agar]]** on the [[top-loading-balance]].
2. Add to a bottle with **100 mL distilled water**.
3. Swirl to disperse (it will not dissolve until heated).
4. **Loosen the cap** (do not seal tight; pressure must equalize during autoclaving).
5. Autoclave on liquid cycle (121C, 15-20 min). See [[autoclave]].
6. After autoclaving, swirl gently. The agar should be fully dissolved and the solution slightly viscous but pourable.
7. Let cool to room temperature before use with seeds.

## Storage

- Room temperature. Stable for weeks.
- If it solidifies (unlikely at 0.1%, but possible if stored cold), microwave briefly with the cap loosened to re-liquefy.

## Labeling

Label the bottle:
- `0.1% Agar`
- Date prepared
- Your initials
- `sterile`
