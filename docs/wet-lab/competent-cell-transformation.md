# Competent Cell Transformation

1. Thaw Competent Cells on ice (\~15 min)
2. Aliquot 50 µl into 4 tubes
3. Mix 2 µl of plasmid and 50 µl of CC by tapping/flicking
4. Keep on ice for 20 min
5. Heat shock by placing the bottom 1/2 to 2/3 of the tube 42°C incubator for 30-60 sec
6. Place tubes on ice for 2 min
7. Add LB (no antibiotic) (\~800 µl), shake at 37C for 45 min
8. Spin-down the CC to collect the cells (\~1 min at 8000 rpm)
9. Remove \~700 µl of supernatant
10. Plate all the cells on 0.7% Agar-LB + antibiotic plates
11. Place plates in 37C incubator
