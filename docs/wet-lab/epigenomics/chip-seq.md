Preparation

- Using the larger LN2-chilled mortar, grind the samples to fine powder (grind with your full-power for 90 sec!), place the powder to LN2-chilled 50 ml tube, and keep the powder in the LN2.

Procedure

- Prep EGS solution by mixing EGS 17 mg x sample / DMSO 245 µl x sample
- Add the following to Nuclei isolation buffer mixture

| added? |  | Per sample | x sample number |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| ◻︎ | EGS/DMSO | 250 µl |  |
| ◻︎ | 2-mercaptoethanol | 25 µl |  |

- Aliquot 25 ml each to 50 ml tube x sample number
- Pour the plant tissue powder to the nuclei isolation buffer tube. Immediately invert gently to mix.
- Place the mixture on a rotator (rpm:             ) for 10 min in RT
- Add 1.56 ml  of formaldehyde per 25 ml of sample
- Place the mixture on a rotator (rpm:             ) for 10 min in RT
- Take the 50 ml tube out of rotator and add the aliquoted 1.7 ml Glycine
- Rotate for another 5 min in RT
- Filter through 70 µl filter into a new 50 ml tube
- Filter through 45 µl filter into a new 50 ml tube
- Centrifuge at 3,000 g, 4 ℃, 10 min
- Remove the supernatant by carefully decanting & pipetting out
- Resuspend in 300 µl of nuclei isolation buffer, by gently pipetting 25 times on ice
- To new 1.5 ml tubes on ice, add 500 µl of nuclei isolation buffer
- Layer the resuspended nuclei solution on top, gently.
- Centrifuge at 4000 g, 4℃ for 10 min
- Remove the supernatant
- Add sample amount (g) / 0.25 x 300 µl of x1 RIPA-Triton+Complete, and pipet on ice for 10 times
- Aliquot 300 µl to 1.5 ml Bioruptor tubes (to achieve approx. 0.25 g sample / tube). Label the cap, keep on ice
- Sonicate @ bioruptor

Menu: 30 sec ON/ 30 sec OFF, x 6 cycle. Make sure to balance!

- To new 1.5 ml tubes, concatenate the aliquoted samples.
- Add  50 µl of 20 % Triton per 1 ml sample. Invert 25 times on ice to mix
- Centrifuge at 13000 g x 4 ℃, 3 min
- Take 30 µl of supernatant to a new tube (Fragmentation check)
- Take the other supernatant to a new low retention tube, flash-freeze with LN2 and keep in the -80℃

Clean-ups:

- If you don’t plan to use bioruptor for consecutive days, exchange the cooling water with bottled DI water.
- Nuclei isolation buffer contains B-me, thus the liquid is a hazardous waste.

### 1.1 Fragmentation Check

- Add 1 µl of RNase (10 mg/ml) to a Fragmentation check sample, incubate 37℃ x 30 min
- Add 1 µl of Proteinate K (20 mg/ml), incubate at 65 ℃ O/N to remove crosslinks, taping the cap to avoid evaporation.
- Use PCI or other DNA-purification protocol to purify DNA. Elute in 10 µl
- Add dye and run the gel (1.5 % gel, 30 min x 100 V)

# 2. Immunoprecipitation

### Day1: night

Materials

- M2 FLAG Antibody (lot:                )
- Frozen chromatin
- Rotator
- A bucket of ice

Procedure

Thaw the chromatin on ice and add 1 µl (1 mg/ml) of antibody per sample

Place the chromatin-antibody mix on rotator and gently rotate at 4℃ O/N

Chill the TE, PBS and water for tomorrow

### Day2

Materials

- Protein G beads (lot:               )
- x2 RIPA solution
- LNDET
- X1 Low salt RIPA + Complete
- X1 Medium salt RIPA + Complete
- X1 High salt RIPA + Complete
- TE
- PBS
- Low retention tube
- Magnetic stand
- A bucket of ice

Procedure

- Gently resuspend the ProteinG beads and take 60 µl x sample to a new 1.5 ml tube on ice
- Place the ProteinG on magnetic stand. Remove supernatant
- Add 1 ml of PBS, gently pipet to mix. Place on magnetic stand. Remove supernatant.
- Add 1 ml of PBS, gently pipet to mix. Place on magnetic stand. Remove supernatant.
- Add 1 ml of Low salt RIPA, gently pipet to mix. Place on magnetic stand. Remove supernatant.
- Add 60 µl x sample of Low salt RIPA and gently pipet to resuspend.
- Remove the samples from the rotator and place on ice. Add 60 µl of washed beads/sample
- Rotate at 4 ℃ x 2 hours
- Place the sample on magnetic stand on ice. Remove supernatant.
- Add 1 ml of Low salt RIPA + Complete. Rotate at 4℃ for 10 min
- Place the sample on magnetic stand on ice. Remove supernatant.
- Add 1 ml of Medium salt RIPA + Complete. Rotate at 4℃ for 10 min
- Place the sample on magnetic stand on ice. Remove supernatant.
- Add 1 ml of High salt RIPA + Complete. Rotate at 4℃ for 10 min
- Change tube by moving all solution to the new low retention tube, pre-chilled on ice.
- Place the sample on magnetic stand on ice. Remove supernatant.
- Add 1 ml of TE. Rotate at 4℃ for 10 min
- Place the sample on magnetic stand on ice. Remove supernatant.
- Add 100 µl of ChIP direct elution buffer. Incubate at 65 ℃ O/N, taping the cap to avoid evaporation.
- Purify DNA with Monrarch DNA cleanup kit. Elute with 15 µl of EB. Measure the DNA concentration with Qubit HS kit
