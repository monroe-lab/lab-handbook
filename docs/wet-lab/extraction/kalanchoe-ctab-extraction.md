CTAB Protocol for Kalanchoe

1. Grind 1 g -2 g (measure first into 15ml falcon tubes) of LN-frozen tissue to fine powder with a [[mortar-and-pestle]].
2. Transfer tissue to 50 ml falcon tubes and immediately add 20 ml of pre-warmed lysis buffer and 150 μL of proteinase K (NextBio, Russia) to the samples and incubate at 60°C for at least 1 h.
3. Add an equal volume of chloroform:isoamyl alcohol (24:1) and [[centrifuge]] at 4000 xg for 15 min to separate the phases.
4. Transfer the upper aqueous layer (containing DNA) to fresh 50ml tubes. Note the volume transferred. Repeat step 2 with the new volume.
5. Transfer the upper aqueous layer to (multiple) fresh 2 ml tubes equally (900 ul each). Add an equal volume of C:I to the tubes and centrifuge at 12000 xg for 5 min. Transfer the aqueous layers to fresh 2 ml tubes. Note the volume transferred. 
6. Add 400 ul of 3M sodium acetate and equal volume (~700-800ul) of ice-cold isopropanol to precipitate the DNA. Invert to mix. Incubate at -20°C for 1h to overnight.
7. Centrifuge at 12000 xg at 4°C for 5-10 minutes to pellet the DNA.
8. Remove the supernatant. A pellet may not be visible for the first precipitation step. Leave some solution in the tube (~200ul), resuspend the sample, and pool all samples together into a total of 4 x 2 ml tubes. Centrifuge again for 5 minutes.
9. Remove the supernatant.
10. Wash the pellet containing DNA with 1ml of ice-cold 75% ethanol. Invert the tube and centrifuge for 5 minutes. Repeat the washing step twice. Leave the pellet to dry for 1-2 minutes or until the ethanol has sufficiently precipitated.
11. Resuspend the DNA pellet in 25 ul of preheated (50°C) EB buffer. (If it is difficult to dissolve the pellet completely: Incubate samples for 10–15 min at 50°C.)
12. Pool the 4 samples into a single tube. => 100ul of total DNA extracted.
13. Add 2ul of RNase A to the DNA and incubate at 37°C for 1h.

---

Kalanchoe samples require additional purification steps:

(A) Bead Purification:

Equilibrate beads to RT before use.

1. Add 1.8X volume of beads to the DNA sample and mix by pipetting 10 times. Incubate at RT for 30-60 minutes to allow beads to bind.
2. Place on a magnetic stand until the solution becomes clear.
3. Remove the cleared solution from the reaction and discard. (Leave 3-5 μL of supernatant behind, otherwise beads may be drawn out with the supernatant.)
4. With tubes still on the stand, wash with 1ml of 75% EtOH and mix by inverting the tubes. Incubate for 30 seconds at room temperature and return to the magnet. Wait for the solution to clear before discarding the ethanol. Repeat for an additional wash.
5. With tubes still on the stand, dry the beads for 1-3 minutes to remove any remaining ethanol. Tubes can be briefly spun to pull down excess ethanol and removed with a P2 pipette.
6. Remove from the stand and add 40 μL of pre-heated elution buffer (EB) at 50°C. Incubate at 37°C for 15 minutes.
7. Place the tube on the magnetic stand to clear then transfer the eluate to a fresh 1.5ml tube.

(B) Monarch PCR & DNA Spin-Column Purification of libraries.

1. Dilute the sample with DNA Cleanup Binding Buffer (1:2) => 100ul DNA: 200ul Binding Buffer

Mix well by pipetting up and down or flicking the tube. Do not vortex. Using a sample volume of 20-100 μl is recommended. For samples less than 20 μl, adjust the volume with TE or nuclease-free water to 20-100 μl. For diluted samples larger than 800 μl, load 800 μl first, proceed with step 2, and repeat as needed.

2. Insert column into collection tube and load sample onto column. Spin at 16,000 xg for 1 minute, then discard the flow-through.
3. Re-insert the column into the collection tube. Add 200 μl of DNA Wash Buffer and spin for 1 minute. Discarding flow-through is optional.
4. Repeat wash (step 3).
5. Transfer the column to a clean 1.5 ml microfuge tube. Use care to ensure that the tip of the column does not touch the flow-through. If in doubt, re-spin for 1 minute.
6. Add >= 6 μl  (20ul) of pre-warmed EB Buffer (50C) to the center of the matrix. Incubate column at RT for 5 minutes, and spin for 1 minute to elute.

Measure DNA on Qubit and [[nanodrop]].
