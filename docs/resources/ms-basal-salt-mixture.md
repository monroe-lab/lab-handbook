# MS Basal Salt Mixture

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Pre-mixed dry blend of macronutrient and micronutrient salts (NH₄NO₃, KNO₃, CaCl₂, MgSO₄, KH₂PO₄, plus iron and trace elements) formulated by Murashige and Skoog in 1962.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The standard mineral nutrient base for plant tissue culture and Arabidopsis seedling plates; combined with sucrose, vitamins, and agar to grow sterile plants.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal lab handling.

Murashige & Skoog medium. 2 bottles.
