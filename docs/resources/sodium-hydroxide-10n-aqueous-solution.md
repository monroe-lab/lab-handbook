# Sodium hydroxide 10N aqueous solution

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Aqueous 10 N NaOH (≈40% w/w); strong base that fully dissociates and absorbs atmospheric CO₂ over time, drifting in concentration if left uncapped.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Ready-to-use base for pH titration of buffer stocks, alkaline lysis Buffer 2 (with SDS) in plasmid preps, and general pH adjustment when you don't want to weigh pellets.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Strongly caustic — splashes can cause permanent eye damage; goggles and gloves required.
