# Bovine Serum Albumin (50 mg/mL)

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A 66 kDa globular serum protein from cow blood, very soluble in water and stable across a wide pH range. The molecule has many surface lysines and a hydrophobic pocket that binds fatty acids and small ligands.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Generic blocker and stabilizer: prevents enzymes and DNA from sticking to plastic, blocks nonspecific antibody binding in Western/ELISA, and stabilizes dilute restriction enzymes. The 50 mg/mL stock is also recommended as a Flongle loading additive to improve nanopore translocation.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal lab handling; treat as a potential biological allergen.

50 mg/mL BSA. Optional Flongle loading buffer additive recommended by Oxford to improve translocation and pore stability. Used in [[flongle-sequencing-and-analysis]].
