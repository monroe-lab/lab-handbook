# Tetracycline hydrochloride

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A four-ring (tetracyclic) polyketide antibiotic, sold as the HCl salt for water solubility; binds the bacterial 30S ribosome and blocks aminoacyl-tRNA loading.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Selection antibiotic for plasmids carrying the tetR resistance marker; standard working concentration ~10–15 μg/mL in LB. Stocks made in 70% ethanol, stored at -20°C, and protected from light because tetracycline photodegrades.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Light- and air-sensitive; harmful if swallowed or inhaled, and a developmental hazard — gloves, dust mask when weighing.
