# 3-Methylbutanol

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Isoamyl alcohol, (CH₃)₂CHCH₂CH₂OH, a branched 5-carbon alcohol that is sparingly water-soluble and floats on aqueous solutions.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The "IAA" in 24:1 chloroform:isoamyl alcohol used during phenol/chloroform DNA extractions; it suppresses foaming and helps cleanly separate the organic and aqueous phases.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Flammable liquid (flash point ~43°C) and a respiratory/skin irritant; handle in a fume hood.
