# Casein Hydrolysate

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> An enzymatic or acid digest of milk casein protein that yields a complex mixture of free amino acids and short peptides.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Added to plant tissue culture media (typically 0.1–1 g/L) as an undefined organic nitrogen source that supplies amino acids and growth factors; also a common ingredient in microbial broths.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal lab handling.
