# Agar (non-TC)

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A linear galactose polysaccharide extracted from red seaweed. When boiled and cooled, the chains form a hydrogen-bonded gel network with melting/setting hysteresis (melts ~85°C, sets ~35°C).

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The standard solidifying agent for microbiology plates (LB agar, YPD, etc.). "Non-TC" grade is fine for bacterial work but is not pure enough for plant tissue culture.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal lab handling; molten agar is hot enough to cause burns.
