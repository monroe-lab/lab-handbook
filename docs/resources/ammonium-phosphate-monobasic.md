# Ammonium phosphate monobasic

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> NH₄H₂PO₄, a water-soluble salt that simultaneously delivers ammonium nitrogen and dihydrogen phosphate. Solutions are mildly acidic (pH ~4.5) and the salt buffers weakly around the H₂PO₄⁻/HPO₄²⁻ pKa.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Combined N + P source in plant nutrition experiments and microbial defined media; also a component of some hydroponic stocks.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal lab handling; mild irritant if dust is inhaled.
