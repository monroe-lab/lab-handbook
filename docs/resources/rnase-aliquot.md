# RNase aliquot

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Single-use frozen aliquots of RNase A, a small endoribonuclease that cleaves single-stranded RNA at the 3' side of pyrimidines; extremely stable and refolds after boiling.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Added to plasmid miniprep resuspension buffer and gDNA preps to chew up contaminating RNA so it doesn't smear gels or inflate A₂₆₀ readings; aliquoted to avoid repeated freeze-thaw and cross-contamination.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard, but a fierce contaminant in any RNA workflow — keep away from RNA bench, tips, and tubes.
