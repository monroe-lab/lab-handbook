# Potassium permanganate

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> KMnO₄, a deep purple strong oxidizer; the permanganate ion (MnO₄⁻) accepts electrons and is reduced to brown MnO₂ or colorless Mn²⁺.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Histological stain and fixative, oxidative cleanup of glassware, and an electron-microscopy contrast agent; in plant work occasionally used for surface sterilization or as a controlled oxidant in chemistry steps.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Strong oxidizer and skin/eye irritant — stains everything purple-brown, keep away from organics and reducers.
