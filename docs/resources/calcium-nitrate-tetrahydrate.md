# Calcium nitrate tetrahydrate

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Ca(NO₃)₂·4H₂O, an ionic salt that delivers both calcium and nitrate ions in solution; highly water-soluble and mildly oxidizing.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Standard calcium and nitrogen source in plant tissue culture media (MS, DKW, and similar formulations) where Ca²⁺ supports cell wall integrity and NO₃⁻ feeds nitrogen metabolism.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Oxidizer; keep away from organic solvents and reducing agents. Mild irritant.
