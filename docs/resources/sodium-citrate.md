# Sodium citrate

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Trisodium salt of citric acid; the citrate ion is a tridentate chelator of divalent cations and a weak buffer around pH 3–6.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The "C" in SSC (saline-sodium citrate), the workhorse hybridization buffer for Southern/Northern blots and FISH; also chelates Ca²⁺ in anticoagulant blood collection.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard.
