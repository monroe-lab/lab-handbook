# Dextran

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A branched polysaccharide of α-1,6-linked glucose subunits with α-1,3 branches; produced by *Leuconostoc* bacteria and sold across a range of defined molecular weights.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> A bulking and crowding agent in molecular biology — used in DNA hybridization buffers (dextran sulfate accelerates kinetics by molecular crowding), in two-phase aqueous separations, and as a size standard for gel filtration.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal lab handling.
