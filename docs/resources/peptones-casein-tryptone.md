# Peptones, casein (Tryptone)

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Casein (milk protein) digested by trypsin into a complex mix of free amino acids and short peptides; rich in nitrogen and growth factors.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The peptide/amino acid component of LB broth, SOB, SOC, and 2xYT media; supplies nitrogen and amino acids that *E. coli* and other bacteria can use directly without making them.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal lab handling; avoid breathing dust when weighing.
