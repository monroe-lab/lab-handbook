# D-Mannitol

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A six-carbon sugar alcohol (the reduced form of mannose); poorly metabolized by most plants and bacteria, so it acts as an inert osmotic agent in solution.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Used to set the osmotic pressure of plant tissue culture media — especially for protoplast isolation, transformation, and embryo rescue — without contributing extra metabolizable carbon.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal lab handling.
