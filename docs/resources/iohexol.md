# Iohexol

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A nonionic, water-soluble iodinated benzene derivative (sold medically as Omnipaque); its high mass and low osmolality let it form steep, gentle density gradients.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Density-gradient medium for separating nuclei, organelles, or cells without the osmotic shock of sucrose or Percoll — common in clean nuclei prep protocols for single-nucleus RNA-seq and ATAC-seq.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard for lab use; avoid dust inhalation when weighing.
