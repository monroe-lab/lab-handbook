# Lithium chloride

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> LiCl, an extremely hygroscopic salt; concentrated solutions are highly chaotropic and disrupt RNA secondary structure.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Selective RNA precipitation (LiCl pellets long RNAs but leaves DNA, tRNAs, and small RNAs in solution); also a mild denaturant for protein extraction.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Harmful if swallowed and an eye irritant; avoid breathing dust when weighing.
