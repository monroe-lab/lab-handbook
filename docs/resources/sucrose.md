# Sucrose

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Disaccharide of glucose and fructose (C₁₂H₂₂O₁₁); highly soluble in water and chemically inert under normal lab conditions.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Osmotic stabilizer in plant protoplast and nuclear isolation buffers, density-gradient medium for organelle and ribosome fractionation, and a carbon source in plant tissue culture media (e.g. MS plates).

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard.
