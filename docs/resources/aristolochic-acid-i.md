# Aristolochic acid I

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A nitro-phenanthrene carboxylic acid natural product from Aristolochia plants. The nitro group is metabolically reduced in vivo to a reactive nitrenium ion that forms covalent DNA adducts, mainly at adenine.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Used as a known mutagen and DNA-damaging agent in mutation-signature and DNA-repair experiments; the lab studies mutational processes, so reference mutagens are useful tools.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Potent nephrotoxin and Group 1 human carcinogen; weigh and handle in a fume hood with double gloves and treat all waste as hazardous.
