# Nuclease-Free Water

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Ultrapure H₂O treated to inactivate or remove DNase and RNase contamination, typically by DEPC treatment followed by autoclaving or by membrane filtration.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Diluent and elution buffer for any workflow where intact nucleic acids matter: PCR setup, qPCR, sequencing library prep, RNA work, and CRISPR guide assembly.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal lab handling.

DNase- and RNase-free water for dilutions, washes, and elutions. Used throughout [[in-house-hifi-shearing-pipeline]].
