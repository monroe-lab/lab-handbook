# 4-Morpholineethanesulfonic acid

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> MES, a Good's zwitterionic buffer with a pKa of ~6.15 at 25°C. The morpholine ring + sulfonate combo gives clean buffering in the slightly acidic range without chelating metals.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Standard buffer for plant tissue culture media (e.g. MS medium adjustments) and for low-pH enzyme reactions where Tris would be too basic.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal lab handling; mild irritant if dust is inhaled.
