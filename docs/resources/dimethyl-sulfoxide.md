# Dimethyl sulfoxide

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> DMSO, (CH₃)₂SO, a polar aprotic solvent that dissolves both polar and nonpolar compounds and crosses biological membranes readily.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Cryoprotectant for freezing cells (typically 5–10%), solvent for stock solutions of poorly water-soluble compounds, and PCR additive that destabilizes secondary structure in GC-rich templates.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Carries dissolved compounds straight through skin — never handle with bare hands, especially after dissolving anything toxic in it.
