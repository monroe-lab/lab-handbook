# Glycerin

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Glycerol, C₃H₈O₃, a viscous trihydroxy alcohol that hydrogen-bonds with water and depresses the freezing point of aqueous mixtures.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Cryoprotectant for bacterial freezer stocks (typically 15–25%), enzyme storage buffers (50%), and density additive in DNA loading dyes; "glycerin" is just the pharma/commercial name for the same molecule.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard — slippery when spilled, otherwise benign.
