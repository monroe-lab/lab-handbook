# Magnesium chloride

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> MgCl₂, very hygroscopic; dissociates fully into Mg²⁺ and 2 Cl⁻ in solution.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Essential cofactor for DNA polymerases, restriction enzymes, and ligases; the [Mg²⁺] in PCR mixes is one of the main knobs for tuning yield and specificity.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal lab handling; mildly irritating to eyes.
