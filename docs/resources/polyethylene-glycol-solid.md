# Polyethylene glycol (solid)

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> PEG is a linear -(CH₂CH₂O)ₙ- polymer; the solid grades (PEG 4000, 6000, 8000) are waxy at room temperature and dissolve readily in water.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Macromolecular crowding agent for ligation reactions, plasmid prep precipitation, protein crystallization, and PEG-mediated cell fusion or transformation.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard; one of the more biocompatible polymers in the lab.
