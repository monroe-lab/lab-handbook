# Tricine

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> N-tris(hydroxymethyl)methylglycine, a zwitterionic Good's buffer with pKa ~8.05 at 25°C — basically Tris with a glycine carboxyl tacked on.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The "T" in tricine-SDS-PAGE, which gives much better resolution of small peptides (<30 kDa) than standard Laemmli glycine-SDS-PAGE; also used in cathode buffers for transfer.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard.
