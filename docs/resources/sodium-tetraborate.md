# Sodium tetraborate

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Na₂B₄O₇ (borax), a weak base whose borate ions form reversible complexes with cis-diols on sugars and polyols.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The "B" in TBE and SB electrophoresis buffers, where borate's low conductivity lets you run gels at higher voltages without overheating; also used in some carbohydrate analysis buffers.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Reproductive toxicant (suspected effects on fertility and development); handle with gloves and avoid dust inhalation.
