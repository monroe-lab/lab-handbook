# Polyvinylpolypyrrolidone (PVPP)

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Crosslinked (insoluble) PVP. Distinct from PVP-40 (soluble). Binds polyphenols and tannins.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Added to CTAB lysis buffer to pull out polyphenols during plant DNA extraction in [[low-pass-1000-sample-sequencing]]. Different reagent from the PVP-40 already in stock — both are sometimes used together.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard.

100 g bottle lasts a long time.
