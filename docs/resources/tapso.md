# TAPSO

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> 3-[N-tris(hydroxymethyl)methylamino]-2-hydroxypropanesulfonic acid, a Good's zwitterionic buffer with pKa ~7.6 at 25°C — basically [[taps|TAPS]] with an extra hydroxyl that drops the pKa.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Near-neutral buffer for enzyme assays and electrophoresis where you need a non-coordinating, low-metal-binding alternative to phosphate.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard.

CAS: 68399-81-5. Solid. Plastic Bottle. Source: EHS inventory
