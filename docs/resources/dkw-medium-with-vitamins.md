# DKW Medium with Vitamins

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Driver and Kuniyuki Walnut medium, a premixed powder of macro- and micronutrient salts plus B-vitamins (thiamine, nicotinic acid, pyridoxine, glycine, myo-inositol) formulated for woody plants.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Standard basal medium for in vitro propagation of walnut, pistachio, and other woody species; just add sucrose, plant growth regulators, and a gelling agent, then autoclave.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal lab handling.
