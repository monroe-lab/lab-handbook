# Lithium hydroxide monohydrate

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> LiOH·H₂O, a strong base that fully dissociates in water but is less soluble than NaOH or KOH.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Used to prepare lithium-counterion buffers (e.g., lithium borate for fast DNA gel runs) and to titrate lithium-containing reagents to specific pH.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Corrosive to skin and eyes; wear gloves and goggles, weigh in a balance hood.
