# Mitomycin C

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A natural product from *Streptomyces caespitosus*; bioreductively activated in cells to form interstrand DNA crosslinks at CpG sequences.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Standard agent for inducing replication-blocking DNA damage in DNA repair and crosslink-repair (Fanconi anemia pathway) studies; also used to mitotically arrest feeder cells.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Highly toxic, carcinogenic, and mutagenic; handle only in a fume hood with double gloves and dispose as hazardous waste.

10 mg vial, store at 2-8C, light-sensitive
