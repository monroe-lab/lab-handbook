# Ethylenediaminetetraacetic acid, GMP

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> EDTA, a hexadentate chelator that wraps around divalent metal ions (Mg²⁺, Ca²⁺, Mn²⁺) from six coordination angles; this "GMP" grade is manufactured to pharma-quality specs.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Inactivates nucleases by sequestering their Mg²⁺/Ca²⁺ cofactors; foundation of TE buffer and a standard component in extraction and stop-reaction buffers.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Eye irritant as dust; otherwise low hazard, but it will inhibit downstream enzymes (PCR, restriction) if it sneaks into reactions.
