# Tris-EDTA (TE) buffer solution

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Standard 10 mM [[trizma-base|Tris]]-HCl + 1 mM [[edta-trisodium-salt|EDTA]] at pH 8.0; the Tris holds the pH and the EDTA chelates Mg²⁺/Ca²⁺ to inactivate trace nucleases.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The default storage buffer for purified DNA and oligos — pH and chelator together keep DNA stable for years at -20°C without nuclease nibbling.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard.
