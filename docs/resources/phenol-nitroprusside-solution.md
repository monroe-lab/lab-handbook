# Phenol nitroprusside solution

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Phenol mixed with sodium nitroprusside in water; in the presence of hypochlorite, ammonia reacts with phenol to form indophenol blue (the Berthelot reaction), and nitroprusside catalyzes that reaction.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Reagent for colorimetric ammonium quantification in soil, plant tissue extracts, and water samples; absorbance at ~640 nm scales with [NH₄⁺].

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Phenol is corrosive and toxic; nitroprusside releases cyanide on decomposition. Handle in fume hood with gloves; dispose as hazardous waste.
