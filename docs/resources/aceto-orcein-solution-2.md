# Aceto-Orcein Solution, 2%

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A 2% solution of orcein (a natural phenoxazone dye from lichens) dissolved in ~45% acetic acid. The acid both fixes tissue and protonates the dye so it binds DNA tightly.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Classic squash-stain for visualizing chromosomes in plant root tips and meiotic cells under brightfield microscopy. Stains chromatin a deep purple while leaving cytoplasm nearly clear.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Acetic acid content makes it corrosive to skin and eyes and gives off pungent vapor; handle in a fume hood with gloves.
