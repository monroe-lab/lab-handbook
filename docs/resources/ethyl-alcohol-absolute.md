# Ethyl Alcohol Absolute

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> C₂H₅OH at ≥99.5% purity ("absolute"), the small two-carbon alcohol fully miscible with water and most polar organic solvents.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The starting stock for diluting working ethanol solutions and the precipitant in nucleic acid clean-ups (2.5 volumes of cold absolute ethanol with salt drops DNA out of solution).

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Flammable, flash point ~13°C; store in a flammables cabinet and keep away from ignition sources.
