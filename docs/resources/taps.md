# TAPS

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> N-tris(hydroxymethyl)methyl-3-aminopropanesulfonic acid, a zwitterionic Good's buffer with pKa ~8.4 at 25°C.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Buffer of choice for slightly alkaline enzymatic work just outside Tris's range, and for protein electrophoresis transfer buffers where Tris would drift too low.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard; mild eye and skin irritant.
