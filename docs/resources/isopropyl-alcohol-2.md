# Isopropyl alcohol

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> (CH₃)₂CHOH, a small secondary alcohol fully miscible with water and most organic solvents.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Used neat for cleaning optics, pipettes, and surfaces, and as a precipitant for nucleic acids (it pellets DNA at lower volumes than ethanol, useful when sample volume is large).

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Flammable, flash point ~12°C; vapor is irritating and intoxicating, keep capped.
