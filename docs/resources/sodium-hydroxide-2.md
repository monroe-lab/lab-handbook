# Sodium hydroxide

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> NaOH, a strong base that fully dissociates in water; the solid is hygroscopic and absorbs CO₂ from air, so weighed amounts are always approximate.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Stock for buffer pH adjustment, alkaline lysis (Buffer 2 with SDS) in plasmid preps, glassware cleaning, and base hydrolysis reactions.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Strongly caustic and exothermic on dissolution — always add solid to water, never the reverse.
