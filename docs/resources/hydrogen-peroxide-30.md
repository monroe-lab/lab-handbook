# Hydrogen Peroxide 30%

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> H₂O₂ at 30% in water — a strong oxidizer that decomposes to water and oxygen, releasing reactive hydroxyl radicals along the way.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Used diluted (typically to ~3–10%) for seed surface sterilization, tissue bleaching, and substrate solutions for HRP-based detection in Westerns and ELISAs.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Corrosive at 30%, will burn skin and bleach clothing on contact; store cold and away from organics and metals to prevent runaway decomposition.

20-35% concentration, handle with care
