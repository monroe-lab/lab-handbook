# Hydrochloric Acid (HCl)

## What it is

Strong mineral acid. Available as concentrated (~12 M / 37%) and dilute (1 M) stocks. Fully dissociates in water.

## Why we use it

pH adjustment: titrating Tris buffers down from their naturally high pH (~10-11) to the working pH (usually 8.0). Used in [[ph-measurement]], [[make-buffer-a]].

> ⚠️ **Corrosive**
> Concentrated HCl produces irritating fumes. Work in the [[fume-hood]] when handling concentrated stock. Wear [[nitrile-gloves]] and safety glasses. Store in the [[cabinet-corrosive]].

CAS: 7647-01-0.
