# Formamide

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> HCONH₂, a small polar amide that disrupts hydrogen bonding between nucleic acid bases, lowering the melting temperature of duplex DNA and RNA.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Denaturant in nucleic acid hybridization (Northern blots, FISH, in situ hybridization) and a loading buffer component for sequencing and RNA gels — keeps strands single and probes specific.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Reproductive toxin and teratogen; handle in a fume hood with gloves, never on the open bench.
