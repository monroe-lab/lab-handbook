# L-Glutamine

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Amide-side-chain amino acid (C₅H₁₀N₂O₃); thermally and hydrolytically unstable in solution, breaks down to ammonia and pyroglutamate.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Nitrogen source and amino acid supplement in plant tissue culture and microbial media; added fresh because it degrades during autoclaving.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal lab handling.
