# Glycerol, 50% aqueous solution

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A 1:1 mix of glycerol (C₃H₈O₃) and water — pre-diluted so it pipettes cleanly without the syrup-like viscosity of pure glycerol.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Convenient working stock for making bacterial freezer stocks (mix 1:1 with overnight culture for a final 25% glycerol) and for diluting enzymes into long-term storage buffers.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard.

CAS: 56-81-5. Liquid. Glass Bottle. Source: EHS inventory
