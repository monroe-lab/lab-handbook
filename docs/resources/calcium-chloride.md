# Calcium chloride

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> CaCl₂, an ionic salt that dissociates fully in water to give Ca²⁺ and Cl⁻; the anhydrous form is strongly hygroscopic and exothermic on dissolution.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Source of Ca²⁺ ions for making chemically competent *E. coli* (the classic Mandel-Higa method) and as a calcium supplement in plant tissue culture media.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Eye and skin irritant; dissolution releases heat, so add slowly to water.
