# Sodium Hydroxide, 10.0 Normal

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Pre-made 10 N (10 M) aqueous NaOH; saves you from weighing solid pellets and the strongly exothermic dissolution step.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Convenient stock for pH adjustment and titration when buffering Tris, MOPS, HEPES, or other acidic stocks up to working pH; small volumes go a long way.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Strongly caustic — causes severe burns; wear goggles and gloves and add slowly when titrating.
