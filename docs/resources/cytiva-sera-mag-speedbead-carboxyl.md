# Sera-Mag SpeedBead Carboxylate-Modified Magnetic Beads

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Paramagnetic carboxylate-coated beads — the raw bead used to formulate homemade SPRI (Rohland & Reich 2012).

> 💡 **Lab use**
> At 2000-reaction scale, homemade SPRI from Sera-Mag + PEG 8000 + NaCl + Tris + EDTA replaces $5-10K of AMPure for [[low-pass-1000-sample-sequencing]].

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard; contains sodium azide as preservative.

15 mL stock makes liters of working SPRI.
