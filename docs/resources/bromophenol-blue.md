# Bromophenol Blue

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A sulfonephthalein dye that is yellow below pH ~3 and deep blue above pH ~4.6, with a small molecular weight (~670 Da) that makes it migrate near the leading edge of small DNA fragments and proteins in a gel.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The blue tracking dye in DNA loading buffer and SDS-PAGE sample buffer — lets you watch the gel run and judge when to stop. Also used as a pH indicator in some buffers.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal lab handling; will stain skin and benches if spilled.
