# Ficoll 400

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A neutral, highly branched sucrose-epichlorohydrin copolymer with an average MW of 400 kDa; dissolves in water without changing ionic strength.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Density and viscosity additive — used in DNA loading dye to weigh samples down into gel wells, and to build density gradients for separating cells, nuclei, or organelles.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard.
