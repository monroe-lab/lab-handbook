# Sodium bicarbonate

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> NaHCO₃, a mild base that buffers around pH 6.3 (carbonic acid) and 10.3 (carbonate); dissolves with slight CO₂ release when acidified.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Buffers cell-culture media (with 5% CO₂ headspace), neutralizes acid spills, and is a component of carbonate/bicarbonate coating buffers for ELISA plates.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard.
