# IPTG (Isopropyl β-D-1-thiogalactopyranoside)

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Non-hydrolyzable lactose analog that induces T7/lac-driven protein expression by binding the lac repressor.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Induces Tn5 expression from pTXB1 in BL21(DE3) during in-house Tn5 purification for [[low-pass-1000-sample-sequencing]].

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard; store dry at -20 °C.

25 g bottle is a lifetime supply.
