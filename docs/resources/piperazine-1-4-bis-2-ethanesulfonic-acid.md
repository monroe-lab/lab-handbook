# Piperazine-1,4-bis(2-ethanesulfonic acid)

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Better known as PIPES, a zwitterionic Good's buffer with a piperazine core and two sulfonate arms; pKa ~6.8 at 25°C.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Buffers slightly-acidic biochemistry (cell lysis, fixation, tubulin polymerization) where Tris is too basic and phosphate would precipitate divalent cations.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard; mild eye/skin irritant in solid form.
