# Spermidine

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A small triamine (H₂N-(CH₂)₃-NH-(CH₂)₄-NH₂), positively charged at physiological pH so it binds tightly to the phosphate backbone of nucleic acids.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Stabilizes DNA structure and boosts activity of many DNA-binding enzymes; standard additive in restriction digests, in vitro transcription, and CUT&RUN/CUT&Tag wash buffers.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Corrosive liquid with a strong amine odor; wear gloves and work in a fume hood when weighing or pipetting concentrated stock.
