# N,N-Dimethylformamide (DMF), Anhydrous

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Polar aprotic solvent. Anhydrous, sealed under inert gas.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Used in some Tn5 purification protocols as a co-solvent for resuspending specific buffer components. Confirm whether the chosen Tn5 protocol in [[low-pass-1000-sample-sequencing]] actually needs DMF vs DMSO before ordering — these are NOT interchangeable.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Reproductive toxin, highly toxic by skin absorption. Use in fume hood with nitrile gloves.

1 L bottle — small order, confirm need first.
