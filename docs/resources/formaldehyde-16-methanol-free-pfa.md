# Formaldehyde (16% methanol free) PFA

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> HCHO, the simplest aldehyde, here supplied as freshly depolymerized paraformaldehyde at 16% in water without methanol stabilizer — it crosslinks proteins and DNA via methylene bridges to nearby amines.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Methanol-free PFA is the gold standard fixative for ChIP, CUT&RUN, immunostaining, and any application where methanol would dehydrate cells or destroy epitopes; sealed ampules give a clean, single-use dose.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Toxic, sensitizer, and a confirmed human carcinogen — work in a fume hood, wear nitrile gloves, and quench waste with Tris or glycine.
