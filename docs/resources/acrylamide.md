# Acrylamide

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> CH₂=CHCONH₂, a small vinyl monomer that polymerizes via free-radical addition (initiated by APS/TEMED) into long polyacrylamide chains.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Cross-linked with bis-acrylamide to cast polyacrylamide gels for protein SDS-PAGE and high-resolution nucleic acid electrophoresis. Pore size is tuned by total %T and %C.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Potent neurotoxin and probable human carcinogen in monomer form; weigh in a fume hood with gloves and prefer pre-made liquid stocks when possible.
