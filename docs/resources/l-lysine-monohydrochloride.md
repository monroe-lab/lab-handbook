# L-Lysine monohydrochloride

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Basic amino acid with a positively charged ε-amino side chain, sold as the HCl salt for stable, water-soluble crystals.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Amino acid supplement in defined growth media and a coating agent for slides (poly-L-lysine adsorbs to glass and gives cells a positive surface to stick to).

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal lab handling.
