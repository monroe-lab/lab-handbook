# 1 kb+ Fast DNA Ladder

DNA size standard for agarose gel electrophoresis. Load in one lane alongside your samples to estimate the size of your DNA bands. Bands range from ~100 bp to 10+ kb with known sizes marked on the datasheet.

Used in [[gel-electrophoresis]], [[pcr-genotyping]], [[gel-imaging-and-annotation]].

Store at -20C.
