# Methyl methanesulfonate

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> CH₃SO₂OCH₃ ("MMS"), an alkylating agent that transfers methyl groups to nucleophilic sites on DNA, primarily N7-guanine and N3-adenine.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The standard chemical mutagen for studying DNA damage response and repair pathways; used to induce alkylation lesions in plant, yeast, and animal cells at controlled doses.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Carcinogen, mutagen, and acutely toxic; handle only in a fume hood with double gloves and dispose as hazardous waste.
