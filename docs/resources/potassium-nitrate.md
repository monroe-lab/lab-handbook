# Potassium nitrate

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> KNO₃, a highly water-soluble salt that dissociates to K⁺ and NO₃⁻; an oxidizer in dry form.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Nitrogen and potassium source in plant growth media (Hoagland's, MS variants) and seed germination assays where nitrate signaling matters; also used to break seed dormancy in some species.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Strong oxidizer — keep away from organic material, fuels, and reducing agents.
