# Carbon, activated

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Elemental carbon processed to have a huge internal surface area (hundreds to thousands of m²/g) of micropores that physically adsorb organic molecules and many ions.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Added to plant tissue culture media to soak up phenolics, browning compounds, and inhibitory exudates released by wounded explants; also used to darken media for root induction.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard; fine dust is a nuisance respiratory irritant, so weigh carefully.
