# Potassium hydroxide

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> KOH, a strong base that fully dissociates in water; dissolution is highly exothermic.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> pH adjustment when you want to add hydroxide without sodium (e.g., K⁺-based buffers); also used in quick plant tissue maceration for chromosome spreads and as an electrolyte in some assays.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Strongly caustic — wear goggles and gloves, and always add solid to water (never the reverse) to control the heat of dissolution.
