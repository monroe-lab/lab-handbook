# Carbenicillin disodium salt

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A semi-synthetic β-lactam antibiotic in the penicillin family, sold as the disodium salt for water solubility; inhibits bacterial cell wall transpeptidases.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Selection marker for *bla* (ampR) plasmids in *E. coli* cloning, and the standard antibiotic for killing *Agrobacterium* after plant transformation since it's more stable than ampicillin and less toxic to plant tissues.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Possible sensitizer; people with penicillin allergies should avoid weighing it.
