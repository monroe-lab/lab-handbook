# Lithium acetate dihydrate

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> CH₃COOLi·2H₂O, a highly water-soluble salt of the smallest alkali metal cation.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Core reagent in the LiAc/PEG yeast transformation method; the Li⁺ ion permeabilizes the yeast cell wall enough to let plasmid DNA enter.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Mild irritant; lithium salts are reproductive toxins at chronic exposure, handle with gloves.
