# Silwet L-77

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A trisiloxane nonionic surfactant with a polyether tail; lowers water surface tension dramatically (down to ~20 mN/m), letting droplets spread and penetrate hydrophobic surfaces.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The wetting agent in the classic Arabidopsis floral-dip transformation — added to the Agrobacterium suspension so it coats the waxy flower buds and reaches developing ovules; also used for foliar spray applications.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Eye and skin irritant; avoid inhaling mist when spraying.
