# HEPES

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> 4-(2-hydroxyethyl)-1-piperazineethanesulfonic acid, a zwitterionic Good's buffer with pKa 7.5 at 25°C and minimal temperature drift compared to Tris.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Workhorse buffer for cell culture, nuclei isolation, and any near-physiological pH (7.2–7.6) work where Tris would shift too much with temperature; doesn't bind divalent cations the way phosphate does.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard; mild irritant as a powder.
