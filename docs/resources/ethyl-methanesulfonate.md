# Ethyl methanesulfonate

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> CH₃SO₂OC₂H₅, a small alkylating agent that transfers an ethyl group onto guanine bases in DNA, producing G→A transitions.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Classic chemical mutagen for forward-genetic screens in plants and microbes — soak seeds or treat cultures, then look for phenotypes in the next generation.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Potent mutagen and suspected carcinogen; handle in a fume hood with double gloves and decontaminate spills with sodium thiosulfate.
