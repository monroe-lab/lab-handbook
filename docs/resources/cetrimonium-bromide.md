# Cetrimonium bromide

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> CTAB, a cationic quaternary ammonium detergent with a 16-carbon tail; positively charged head group binds anionic molecules and disrupts membranes.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The detergent in CTAB DNA extraction buffer, the workhorse method for plant genomic DNA — it lyses cells and selectively complexes with polysaccharides and polyphenols so they partition away from DNA during chloroform extraction.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Harmful if inhaled or swallowed; weigh in a fume hood and avoid generating dust.
