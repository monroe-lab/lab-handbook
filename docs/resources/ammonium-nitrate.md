# Ammonium nitrate

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> NH₄NO₃, a highly water-soluble salt that supplies both reduced (NH₄⁺) and oxidized (NO₃⁻) nitrogen. Dissolution is strongly endothermic.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The major nitrogen source in MS plant tissue culture medium and many hydroponic recipes; the dual-form nitrogen supports plant growth better than either ion alone.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Strong oxidizer — keep away from organics, fuels, and reducing agents; explosive risk in bulk or when contaminated, and irritant to skin/eyes.
