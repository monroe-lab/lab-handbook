# Agar, Plant, TC

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A high-purity galactose polysaccharide from red seaweed, washed to remove inhibitory salts, phenolics, and trace metals that the plain microbiology grade still contains.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The solidifying agent for plant tissue culture media (MS plates, callus induction, regeneration). The TC grade matters because Arabidopsis and other plant cells are sensitive to the contaminants left in non-TC agar.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal lab handling; molten agar is hot enough to cause burns.
