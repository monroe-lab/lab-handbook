# Proteinase K (Sigma P2308)

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Broad-specificity serine protease from Tritirachium album. 100 mg lyophilized powder reconstituted to ~20 mg/mL working stock.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Digests protein during plant DNA extraction (both CTAB and DNeasy 96 workflows) in [[low-pass-1000-sample-sequencing]]. We don't currently have bulk Proteinase K listed as a reagent.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard as powder; avoid inhalation. Working solutions stable at -20 °C.

100 mg bottle is enough for many thousands of 96-well extractions.

**Procurement:** Millipore Sigma punch-out. Vendor: Millipore Sigma. Catalog: P2308.
