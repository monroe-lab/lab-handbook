# Benzonase Nuclease

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Engineered endonuclease from Serratia marcescens; digests all forms of DNA and RNA to 3-5 nt fragments.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Clears host nucleic acids during Tn5 lysate preparation in [[low-pass-1000-sample-sequencing]], reducing viscosity and removing copurifying E. coli DNA.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard; store at -20 °C.

10 kU bottle.
