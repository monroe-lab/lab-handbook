# Sodium hydroxide

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> NaOH, a strong base that fully dissociates in water; pellets are hygroscopic and rapidly absorb moisture and CO₂ from air.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Buffer pH adjustment, alkaline lysis Buffer 2 (with SDS) in plasmid miniprep, base for hydrolysis reactions, and glassware/column regeneration.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Strongly caustic; dissolution is exothermic, so always add solid slowly to water (never water to solid) and wear goggles.
