# Mineral oil

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A mixture of long-chain saturated hydrocarbons from petroleum refining; chemically inert, hydrophobic, and non-volatile.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Overlay for PCR reactions in older thermocyclers without heated lids, evaporation seal for small-volume long incubations, and as an inert immersion fluid for some microscopy.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal lab handling; aspiration into lungs is harmful, do not pipette by mouth.
