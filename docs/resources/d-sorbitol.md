# D-Sorbitol

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A six-carbon sugar alcohol, isomeric with mannitol, that is highly water-soluble and minimally metabolized by most cultured plant cells.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Osmotic agent for plant tissue culture and protoplast work; also a common cryoprotectant and stabilizer for enzymes and membranes during freezing.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal lab handling.
