# Hexadecyltrimethyl-ammonium bromide

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> CTAB, a cationic detergent with a 16-carbon tail and a permanently positive trimethylammonium head group; binds and precipitates polysaccharides and acidic proteins at low salt.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The active ingredient in CTAB plant DNA extraction buffer — strips membranes, neutralizes negatively charged contaminants, and leaves clean genomic DNA in solution after a chloroform partition.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Harmful if inhaled or swallowed and irritating to skin and eyes; weigh in a fume hood or balance enclosure.
