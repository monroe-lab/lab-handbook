# Triton X-100

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A non-ionic detergent: octylphenol head with an ~9.5-unit polyethylene glycol tail; mild enough to solubilize membranes without denaturing most proteins.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Permeabilizes cells and nuclei for immunostaining (typically 0.1–0.5%), solubilizes membrane proteins, and reduces non-specific binding in Western and IP wash buffers.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Eye irritant and aquatic toxicant; viscous liquid that's a pain to pipette accurately — cut tips help.

100 mL + 500 mL bottles
