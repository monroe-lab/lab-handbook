# Bis (N,N'-Methylene-bis-acrylamide)

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Two acrylamide groups linked by a methylene bridge. During free-radical polymerization it cross-links growing polyacrylamide chains, turning a linear polymer into a true 3D gel.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Mixed with [[acrylamide]] (typical 29:1 or 37.5:1 acrylamide:bis ratio) to cast SDS-PAGE and native protein gels. The bis ratio sets the cross-link density and therefore the effective pore size.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Toxic and a probable carcinogen in monomer form; weigh in a fume hood with gloves and prefer pre-made stock solutions.
