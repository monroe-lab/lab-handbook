# TAE Buffer (50x Stock)

## What it is

Tris-Acetate-EDTA buffer, 50x concentrated stock solution. Composition: 2 M Tris, 1 M acetic acid, 100 mM EDTA. Dilute to 1x for use as the running buffer in agarose gel electrophoresis.

## Why we use it

TAE is the standard electrophoresis buffer in this lab. It conducts electricity (ions carry current through the gel), maintains pH ~8.3 during the run (prevents DNA degradation), and the EDTA chelates divalent cations that nucleases need, protecting DNA samples. Used in [[gel-electrophoresis]], [[making-an-agarose-gel]].

Store at room temperature. Stable for at least 1 year.
