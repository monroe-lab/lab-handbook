# Bradford Reagent (Coomassie G-250)

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Coomassie Brilliant Blue G-250 in acidic solution; shifts absorbance from 465 to 595 nm on binding protein.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Quick protein quantification during Tn5 purification fractions in [[low-pass-1000-sample-sequencing]].

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Contains phosphoric acid — corrosive.

500 mL ready-to-use solution.
