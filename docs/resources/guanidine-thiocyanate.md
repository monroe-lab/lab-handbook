# Guanidine thiocyanate

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A chaotropic salt (CH₅N₃·HSCN) that disrupts hydrogen bonds and hydrophobic interactions, instantly denaturing proteins including RNases.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Core ingredient of TRIzol and Qiagen RNA/DNA extraction lysis buffers — kills RNases on contact and lets nucleic acids bind silica columns at high salt.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Harmful if swallowed and a skin/eye irritant; never mix waste with bleach (releases toxic cyanide and HCN gases).
