# 2-Mercaptoethanol

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> HOCH₂CH₂SH, a small thiol-alcohol. The free –SH group is a strong reducing agent that breaks protein disulfide bonds by thiol-disulfide exchange.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Added to CTAB and Laemmli buffers to denature proteins and inactivate nucleases/oxidases during DNA, RNA, and protein extractions. Also used in SDS-PAGE sample buffer to fully unfold proteins.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Acutely toxic, foul-smelling, and volatile; always pipette in a fume hood and double-glove.
