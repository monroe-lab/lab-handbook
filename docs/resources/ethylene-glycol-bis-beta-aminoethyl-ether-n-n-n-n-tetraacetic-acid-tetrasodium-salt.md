# Ethylene glycol-bis(beta-aminoethyl ether)-N,N,N',N'-tetraacetic acid tetrasodium salt

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> EGTA, a tetracarboxylic chelator structurally similar to EDTA but with a much higher affinity for Ca²⁺ over Mg²⁺ thanks to its longer ether-linked backbone.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Used to selectively sequester calcium without stripping magnesium from buffers, e.g. in calcium-signaling experiments and nuclei isolation where Mg²⁺-dependent enzymes need to keep working.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard; mild eye/skin irritant in dust form.
