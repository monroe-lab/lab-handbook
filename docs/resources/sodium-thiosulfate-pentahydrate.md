# Sodium thiosulfate pentahydrate

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Na₂S₂O₃·5H₂O, a mild reducing agent that quickly reacts with halogens and heavy-metal cations to form soluble complexes.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Quenches residual chlorine and iodine (useful when neutralizing bleach in seed sterilization rinses or in water treatment); also a classic iodometric titration standard.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal handling.
