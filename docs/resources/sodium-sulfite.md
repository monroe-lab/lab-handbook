# Sodium sulfite

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Na₂SO₃, a strong reducing agent that scavenges dissolved oxygen and slowly oxidizes to sulfate in air.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Oxygen scavenger and antioxidant in solutions where oxidation would degrade reagents; also reduces disulfide bonds and is used in some DNA modification chemistries (e.g. bisulfite-related workflows).

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Releases SO₂ on contact with acids; irritating to eyes and respiratory tract, and a known sulfite allergen.
