# DNase I, RNase-free (Thermo EN0521)

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Recombinant bovine DNase I, certified RNase-free. Degrades ssDNA and dsDNA.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Cleans up host DNA during Tn5 purification and optionally during some post-tagmentation steps in [[low-pass-1000-sample-sequencing]]. Lab has generic DNase but not specifically Thermo EN0521.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard; -20 °C.

5 kU vial.

**Procurement:** Fisher punch-out. Vendor: Thermo. Catalog: EN0521.
