# Calcium carbonate

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> CaCO₃, a poorly water-soluble inorganic salt (chalk, limestone). Reacts with acids to release CO₂ and acts as a mild base, slowly neutralizing acidic solutions.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Soil/substrate amendment to raise pH for plant growth experiments, and a buffering filler in some chemistry protocols where a slow-release base is desired.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal lab handling; mild dust irritant to eyes and airways.
