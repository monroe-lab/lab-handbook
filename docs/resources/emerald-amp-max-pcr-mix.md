# Emerald Amp MAX PCR Master Mix

2x premixed PCR master mix from TaKaRa containing Taq polymerase, dNTPs, reaction buffer, and loading dye. Just add primers, template DNA, and water. The built-in loading dye means PCR products can be loaded directly onto a gel without adding separate loading dye.

Used for genotyping PCR in [[pcr-genotyping]]. Store at -20C.
