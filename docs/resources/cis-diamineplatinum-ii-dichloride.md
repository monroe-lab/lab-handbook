# cis-Diamineplatinum(II) dichloride

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Cisplatin, a square-planar platinum(II) complex with two chloride and two ammine ligands in cis geometry; in low-chloride environments the chlorides hydrolyze to give a reactive aquated species.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> A DNA-damaging agent that crosslinks adjacent guanines (mostly intrastrand 1,2-d(GpG) adducts); used in research to induce DNA damage and study repair, mutagenesis, and replication stress responses.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Cytotoxic, mutagenic, and a reproductive hazard; handle as a chemotherapeutic in a fume hood with double gloves.
