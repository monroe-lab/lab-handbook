# Potassium chloride

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> KCl, a simple ionic salt that fully dissociates in water to K⁺ and Cl⁻.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Adjusts ionic strength in PCR buffers and protein-binding assays, mimics intracellular K⁺ in physiological buffers, and is the salt component of standard 3M KCl reference electrodes.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard.
