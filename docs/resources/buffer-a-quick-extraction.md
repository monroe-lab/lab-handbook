# Buffer A (Quick Extraction Buffer)

## What it is

Alkaline high-salt extraction buffer. Composition: 100 mM Tris-HCl (pH 9.5), 1 M KCl, 10 mM EDTA. Used in the [[quick-dna-extraction]] protocol for fast, crude DNA extraction from Arabidopsis leaf tissue.

## Why we use it

When heated to 95C, Buffer A lyses plant cells by a combination of high pH (denatures proteins), high salt (1 M KCl precipitates proteins and polysaccharides), and EDTA (chelates Mg2+ to inhibit nucleases). The supernatant contains crude DNA suitable for PCR genotyping but not for sequencing.
