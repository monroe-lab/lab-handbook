# EGTA

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Ethylene glycol tetraacetic acid, a chelator structurally similar to EDTA but with a much higher selectivity for Ca²⁺ over Mg²⁺ (~10⁵-fold).

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Used when you need to strip calcium specifically without disturbing magnesium-dependent enzymes — chromatin and nuclear isolation buffers, kinase reactions, and calcium signaling experiments.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Eye and skin irritant; otherwise low hazard.
