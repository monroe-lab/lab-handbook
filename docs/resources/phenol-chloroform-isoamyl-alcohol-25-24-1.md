# Phenol-Chloroform-Isoamyl alcohol 25:24:1

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Phase-separation mix: phenol denatures and partitions proteins to the organic phase, chloroform improves phase separation and removes lipids, isoamyl alcohol suppresses foaming at the interface.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Classic deproteinization step in genomic and plasmid DNA prep; aqueous phase keeps the nucleic acids while proteins, lipids, and pigments go to the organic/interface.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Phenol is corrosive (severe burns) and toxic; chloroform is a suspected carcinogen. Handle in a fume hood with double gloves and dispose as halogenated waste.
