# Basic Fuchsin

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A magenta triarylmethane dye (a mix of pararosaniline and related homologs) that carries a positive charge and binds anionic groups in cell walls and nucleic acids.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Active stain in Schiff's reagent for the Feulgen reaction (selective DNA staining in plant root tips and pollen) and as a general histological counterstain. Also stains lignin in plant cell walls.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Suspected carcinogen; stains skin and clothing strongly, so wear gloves and a lab coat and clean spills immediately.
