# Isopropyl alcohol, 70% in water

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> 70% (v/v) (CH₃)₂CHOH in water; the water fraction slows evaporation and helps the alcohol penetrate microbial cell membranes before they dry out.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Standard surface and bench disinfectant for tissue culture hoods and benchtops; 70% kills more effectively than 100% because the alcohol can't denature proteins it can't reach.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Flammable; keep away from open flames and Bunsen burners.
