# Chloroform:Isoamyl alcohol 24:1

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A 24:1 v/v premix of chloroform (CHCl₃, dense organic solvent) and isoamyl alcohol; the isoamyl alcohol reduces foaming and stabilizes the protein interface during phase separation.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The organic phase used after phenol in nucleic acid extractions to strip residual phenol and denature proteins, leaving DNA or RNA in the upper aqueous layer.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Chloroform is a suspected carcinogen and CNS depressant; use only in a fume hood with nitrile gloves.
