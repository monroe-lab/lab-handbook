# 1,8-Naphthalic anhydride

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A fused-ring aromatic anhydride (naphthalene with a cyclic dicarboxylic anhydride bridging the 1,8 positions). Reacts readily with amines to form fluorescent naphthalimide derivatives.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Used in specialized chemistry experiments, mainly as a building block for fluorescent dyes/probes and as a herbicide safener in plant biology. See notes for specific protocols.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Skin, eye, and respiratory irritant; weigh in a fume hood and avoid dust inhalation.
