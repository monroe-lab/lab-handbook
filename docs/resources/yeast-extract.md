# Yeast extract

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Water-soluble fraction of autolyzed yeast cells: a complex mix of amino acids, peptides, B vitamins, nucleotides, and trace minerals — not a single defined molecule.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The nitrogen and growth-factor source in LB, YPD, and most other rich bacterial/yeast media; "5 g/L yeast extract" is what makes LB rich rather than minimal.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard.
