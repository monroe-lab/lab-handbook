# Spermine

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A linear tetraamine (one nitrogen more than [[spermidine]]), carrying four positive charges at physiological pH so it wraps DNA even more tightly.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Condenses and protects chromatin in nuclear isolation buffers; common additive in CUT&RUN, CUT&Tag, and ChIP protocols where intact nuclei matter.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Corrosive and a strong amine; gloves, goggles, and fume hood when weighing.
