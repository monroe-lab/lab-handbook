# MES Buffer

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> 2-(N-morpholino)ethanesulfonic acid, a Good's buffer with pKa ~6.15 at 25°C; zwitterionic and non-coordinating to metal ions.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Standard buffer for slightly acidic biology: plant tissue culture media (pH 5.7), enzyme assays in the pH 5.5–6.7 range, and protein purification where Tris would be too basic.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal lab handling; mild eye and skin irritant as the dry powder.
