# Polyvinylpyrrolidone

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> PVP is a water-soluble polymer of N-vinylpyrrolidone monomers; the amide carbonyls hydrogen-bond to phenolic compounds.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Added to plant DNA/RNA extraction buffers (CTAB, etc.) to bind polyphenols and tannins so they don't co-purify and inhibit downstream enzymes; also a blocking agent in hybridization buffers.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard; nuisance dust when weighing.
