# Potassium acetate

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> KCH₃COO, the potassium salt of acetic acid; mildly basic in solution and very water-soluble.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Key ingredient in alkaline lysis miniprep "Buffer 3" — neutralizes the NaOH/SDS lysate and the bulky K⁺ precipitates SDS along with chromosomal DNA and protein, leaving plasmid DNA in solution.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard.
