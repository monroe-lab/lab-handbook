# Glufosinate-ammonium

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A phosphinothricin-based herbicide (ammonium salt) that mimics glutamate and irreversibly inhibits glutamine synthetase, causing ammonia buildup and cell death in plants.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The selection agent for the BAR/PAT resistance marker — used to screen Arabidopsis and other plant transformants on plates or by spraying soil-grown seedlings (commercial name BASTA).

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Acutely toxic if swallowed or inhaled, reproductive toxin; weigh in a hood, double-glove, and avoid spraying without a respirator.
