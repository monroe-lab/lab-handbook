# Cefotaxime sodium salt

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Third-generation cephalosporin antibiotic, a β-lactam that blocks bacterial cell wall synthesis; the sodium salt is freely water-soluble.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Used after *Agrobacterium*-mediated plant transformation to eliminate the bacteria from explants without harming the regenerating plant tissue.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Possible sensitizer; avoid handling if you have a β-lactam allergy.
