# TWEEN 80

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Polysorbate 80, a non-ionic detergent built from a sorbitan core esterified with oleic acid and polyethylene glycol chains; viscous yellow liquid, miscible with water.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Surfactant for plant spray solutions and seed treatments to break surface tension on waxy leaves; also a mild blocking/wash detergent in ELISA and Western workflows (often as 0.05% in PBS-T variants).

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard; mild eye irritant.
