# Ammonium chloride

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> NH₄Cl, a highly water-soluble inorganic salt that releases NH₄⁺ and Cl⁻ ions and gives slightly acidic solutions due to ammonium hydrolysis.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Nitrogen source in defined microbial growth media (e.g. M9 minimal medium) and a component of red blood cell lysis buffers.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Mild irritant to skin, eyes, and respiratory tract; releases ammonia fumes when heated or mixed with strong base.
