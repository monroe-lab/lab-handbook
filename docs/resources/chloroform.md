# Chloroform

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> CHCl₃, a dense (1.49 g/mL) chlorinated organic solvent that's only slightly miscible with water and forms a clean lower phase below aqueous solutions.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The organic extractant in phenol-chloroform DNA/RNA prep — denatures proteins and pulls lipids and phenol out of the aqueous nucleic acid layer.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Suspected carcinogen, hepatotoxin, and CNS depressant; use only in a fume hood and never with acetone (forms explosive byproducts in basic conditions).

ACS grade, use in fume hood only
