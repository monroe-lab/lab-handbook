# IGEPAL CA-630

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A nonionic detergent (octylphenol polyethoxylate) chemically equivalent to NP-40 — a hydrophobic alkyl-phenol head with a short polyethylene glycol tail that gently solubilizes membranes.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Mild lysis detergent for nuclei isolation (typically 0.1–0.5%) — pops the cytoplasmic membrane while leaving the nuclear envelope intact, the standard surfactant in many ChIP, CUT&RUN, and ATAC-seq buffers.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Eye and skin irritant; otherwise low hazard.
