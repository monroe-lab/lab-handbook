# Tween 20 (Polysorbate 20)

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Polysorbate 20 — non-ionic detergent with shorter fatty-acid chain than Tween 80.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Used in Tn5 storage/dilution buffer (typically 0.1%) and in wash buffers for [[low-pass-1000-sample-sequencing]]. Lab has Tween 80 only; Tween 20 is a distinct reagent and the standard choice for enzymatic buffers.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard.

500 mL bottle lasts years.
