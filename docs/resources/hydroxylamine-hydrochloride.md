# Hydroxylamine hydrochloride

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> NH₂OH·HCl, a small reducing/aminating reagent that hydroxylates cytosine residues in DNA, causing C→T transitions.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Classic chemical mutagen for in vitro mutagenesis of plasmid DNA, and a reducing/cleavage reagent in protein chemistry (e.g. cleaving Asn-Gly bonds and reducing oxidized methionines).

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Mutagen, suspected carcinogen, and a skin/eye irritant — handle with gloves in a fume hood and store cool and dry.
