# Rapid Barcoding Kit 24 V14 (SQK-RBK114.24)

Multiplexed nanopore library prep kit (~$700). 6 preps per kit, each prep barcodes up to 24 samples (RB01-RB24) for one Flongle run. The library prep used in [[flongle-rapid-barcoding-rbk114]].

**Do not order alternatives by mistake.** SQK-RAD114 is the single-sample rapid kit (wrong workflow). SQK-RBK114.96 is 96-plex (overkill). The right kit is exactly **SQK-RBK114.24**.
