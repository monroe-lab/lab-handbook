# Gentamicin sulfate salt

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> An aminoglycoside antibiotic complex (mostly gentamicin C1, C1a, C2) that binds the bacterial 30S ribosome and causes mistranslation; the sulfate salt is water-soluble and stable.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Selection antibiotic for plasmids carrying the aacC1 resistance gene, and added to plant tissue-culture media to suppress contaminating bacteria; also used in mammalian cell culture to control gram-negative contamination.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Skin sensitizer and inhalation hazard as a powder; weigh in a hood or balance enclosure and double-glove.
