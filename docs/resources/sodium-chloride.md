# Sodium chloride

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> NaCl, the canonical ionic salt; fully dissociates into Na⁺ and Cl⁻ in water and contributes to ionic strength without changing pH.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The "S" in PBS, SSC, and TBS; sets ionic strength for hybridizations, protein binding, and lysis buffers, and at high concentrations precipitates proteins out of solution (salting out).

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard.
