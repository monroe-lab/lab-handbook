# 1-Octanol

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> C₈H₁₇OH, a straight-chain fatty alcohol. Nearly insoluble in water but freely miscible with organic solvents, which is why its octanol/water partition coefficient (log P) is the standard reference for compound hydrophobicity.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Used as an antifoam additive during plant tissue grinding and DNA extractions, and as a hydrophobic reference solvent in partition assays.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Mild skin/eye irritant; combustible liquid (flash point ~81°C), low acute toxicity.
