# 1-HYDROXY-3-(3-TRIFLUOROMETHYLPHENYL)UREA

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A substituted hydroxyurea bearing a trifluoromethyl-phenyl group. The N-hydroxyurea moiety is a known iron-binder and radical scavenger, and the CF₃ group makes the molecule lipophilic and metabolically stable.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Used in specialized chemistry and biology experiments as a small-molecule probe; see notes for specific protocols.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Treat as an irritant of unknown toxicity; handle with gloves in a fume hood.
