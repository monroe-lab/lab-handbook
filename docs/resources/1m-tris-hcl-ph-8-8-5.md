# 1M Tris-HCL (pH 8-8.5)

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A 1 M stock of tris(hydroxymethyl)aminomethane titrated to pH 8.0–8.5 with HCl. Tris has a pKa near 8.1 at 25°C, so it buffers strongly in this range.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Workhorse stock for diluting into TE, TAE, TBE, lysis buffers, and most enzyme reaction buffers. Pre-made 1 M stocks save time and avoid re-titrating tris base every prep.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal lab handling; pH is temperature-sensitive (drops ~0.028/°C), so titrate at use temperature.
