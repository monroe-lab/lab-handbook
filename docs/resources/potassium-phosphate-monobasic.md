# Potassium phosphate monobasic

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> KH₂PO₄, the acidic partner to K₂HPO₄ in the potassium phosphate buffer system; together they buffer around pKa ~7.2.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Mixed with the dibasic salt to make potassium phosphate buffers (enzyme assays, protein purification, physiological buffers); also a P and K source in plant growth media.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard.
