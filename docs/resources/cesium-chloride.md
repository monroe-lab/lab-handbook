# Cesium chloride

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> CsCl, an ionic salt of the heavy alkali metal cesium; extremely water-soluble and yields very dense solutions (up to ~1.9 g/mL).

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Classic density-gradient ultracentrifugation medium for separating DNA by GC content or for purifying plasmid DNA, plus the historical Meselson-Stahl experiment that proved semi-conservative replication.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Mild irritant; expensive enough that it's worth recovering and recycling solutions.
