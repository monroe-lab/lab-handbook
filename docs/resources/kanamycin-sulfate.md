# Kanamycin Sulfate

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Aminoglycoside antibiotic from *Streptomyces kanamyceticus*; binds the bacterial 30S ribosomal subunit and causes mistranslation.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Selection agent for plasmids carrying the *nptII/kanR* resistance marker, typically at 50 µg/mL in LB for *E. coli* and 50 µg/mL on plant tissue culture media for transgenic Arabidopsis.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Suspected reproductive toxin and irritant; weigh in a balance hood and wear gloves.
