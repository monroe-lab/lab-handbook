# Imidazole hydrochloride

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A small five-membered aromatic ring with two nitrogens (the side chain of histidine), here as the HCl salt; pKa ~7.0 makes it a useful near-neutral buffer.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Competitive eluent in Ni-NTA affinity purification of His-tagged proteins — at low concentrations (~10–20 mM) it reduces nonspecific binding, at high concentrations (~250 mM) it knocks the tagged protein off the column.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Skin/eye irritant and possible reproductive toxin; handle with gloves and avoid breathing dust.
