# Roche cOmplete Protease Inhibitor Cocktail, EDTA-free

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Mini tablets: cocktail of serine, cysteine, and metalloprotease inhibitors. EDTA-free so it doesn't interfere with divalent-cation-dependent enzymes downstream.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Protects Tn5 during lysis and purification in [[low-pass-1000-sample-sequencing]].

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard; store at 4 °C.

Pack of 20 tablets — dissolve in lysis buffer fresh.
