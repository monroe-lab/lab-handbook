# Sodium bisulfite

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> NaHSO₃, a reducing agent and sulfonating reagent; in acidic conditions it deaminates unmethylated cytosines to uracil while leaving 5-methylcytosine intact.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The chemistry behind bisulfite sequencing — converts unmethylated C to T after PCR, so methylation status can be read at single-base resolution; also a general reductant and oxygen scavenger.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Releases SO₂ on acidification; respiratory irritant — weigh in a hood.
