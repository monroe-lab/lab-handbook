# NEB Chitin Resin

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Affinity resin with immobilized chitin; binds chitin-binding-domain-tagged proteins from IMPACT expression systems.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> pTXB1-Tn5 has an intein + CBD tag. Chitin resin is the affinity step in in-house Tn5 purification for [[low-pass-1000-sample-sequencing]]. Self-cleavage on-column releases untagged Tn5.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard.

20 mL slurry — enough for multiple preps.
