# Hydrogen peroxide,  20 to  35 % in water

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> H₂O₂, a strong oxidizer that decomposes to water and oxygen, generating reactive hydroxyl radicals along the way.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Used at low percent for seed surface sterilization and to bleach plant tissue for clearing or in situ work; also a substrate for HRP-based assays (Westerns, ELISAs).

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Corrosive at this concentration, will burn skin and bleach clothing instantly; store in a vented cap container away from organics and metals.
