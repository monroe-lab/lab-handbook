# Reagent Alcohol 100% (200 proof, Denatured)

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Ethanol (C₂H₅OH) at 100% / 200 proof, "denatured" with small amounts of methanol or isopropanol so it can't be consumed and dodges federal beverage-alcohol tax.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Functionally equivalent to absolute ethanol for nucleic acid precipitation, dehydration series, and surface sterilization (diluted to 70%); cheaper than pure ethanol and the standard "lab ethanol" for non-cell-culture work.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Highly flammable (flash point ~13°C); the methanol denaturant adds toxicity, so don't ingest or use on tissue you'll consume.
