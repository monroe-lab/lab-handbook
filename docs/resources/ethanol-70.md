# Ethanol 70%

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> 70% v/v ethanol (C₂H₅OH) in water — the water content is essential because pure ethanol dehydrates the outside of microbial cells too fast and forms a barrier that protects the interior.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Standard surface disinfectant for benches, gloved hands, pipettes, and tools; also the wash for DNA pellets in ethanol precipitation, where it removes residual salt without resolubilizing the DNA.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Flammable (flash point ~17°C); keep away from open flames and store in a flammables cabinet.
