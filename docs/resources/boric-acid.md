# Boric acid

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> H₃BO₃, a weak Lewis acid (pKa ~9.2) that accepts a hydroxide from water rather than donating a proton. Forms transient complexes with cis-diols on sugars and ribose.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The "B" in TBE buffer for nucleic acid gels — it pairs with Tris to give a low-conductivity buffer that runs cool. Also a micronutrient in plant growth media.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Reproductive toxin; avoid inhalation of dust and skin contact, especially for anyone pregnant or trying to conceive.
