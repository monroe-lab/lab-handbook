# Sodium Acetate, Anhydrous

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> NaCH₃COO, the sodium salt of acetic acid; the anhydrous form has no waters of crystallization, so molarity calculations are 1:1 by mass.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The salt half of ethanol DNA/RNA precipitations (3M NaOAc pH 5.2 + 2.5 vol ethanol) — neutralizes phosphate backbone charges so DNA aggregates and pellets; also used to make acetate buffers around pKa 4.76.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard.
