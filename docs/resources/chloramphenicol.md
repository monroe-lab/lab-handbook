# Chloramphenicol

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A small nitroaromatic antibiotic that binds the 50S ribosomal subunit and blocks the peptidyl transferase reaction; poorly soluble in water, so stocks are made in ethanol.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Selection marker for *cat*-bearing plasmids in *E. coli* cloning, typically used at 25–34 µg/mL; also used to amplify low-copy plasmids by stalling chromosomal replication.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Suspected carcinogen and bone marrow toxin; weigh in a fume hood and wear gloves.
