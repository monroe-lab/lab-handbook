# Glycine

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> The smallest amino acid (H₂N-CH₂-COOH), zwitterionic in solution with two pKa values (~2.4 and ~9.6) that make it a useful buffer near pH 9 and pH 2.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The "G" in Tris-glycine SDS-PAGE running buffer and Western transfer buffer; also commonly used to quench excess formaldehyde after crosslinking in ChIP and CUT&RUN.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard.
