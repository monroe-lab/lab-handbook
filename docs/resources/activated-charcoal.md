# Activated charcoal

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Amorphous carbon processed to give an enormous internal surface area (~500–1500 m²/g). The micropores adsorb organic molecules and pigments via van der Waals interactions.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Added to plant tissue culture media to soak up phenolic exudates, growth inhibitors, and excess hormones; also used to decolorize protein and chemical solutions.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard chemically, but the fine dust is a respiratory irritant and a slipping hazard; weigh in a hood with a dust mask.
