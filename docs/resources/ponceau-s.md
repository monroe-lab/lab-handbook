# Ponceau S

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> A red diazo dye with sulfonate groups that bind reversibly to the positively charged amine groups of proteins on a membrane.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Quick reversible total-protein stain for nitrocellulose or PVDF Western blots; used to confirm transfer worked and to check loading before antibody probing, then washed off with TBS.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard; stains skin and clothing.
