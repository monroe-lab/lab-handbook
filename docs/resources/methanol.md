# Methanol

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> CH₃OH, the smallest alcohol, fully miscible with water and most organic solvents; lower boiling point (65°C) than ethanol.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Mobile phase for HPLC and LC-MS, fixative for cells on slides (denatures proteins and dehydrates), and solvent for hydrophobic small molecules and stains.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Highly flammable and acutely toxic if ingested or absorbed through skin (metabolizes to formaldehyde, can cause blindness); always wear gloves and use a fume hood.
