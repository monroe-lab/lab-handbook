# DTT (Dithiothreitol)

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Small-molecule reducing agent that keeps cysteines reduced during protein purification.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Added to Tn5 lysis and storage buffers during in-house purification in [[low-pass-1000-sample-sequencing]].

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Skin/eye irritant; store powder at 4 °C, aqueous solutions at -20 °C (unstable at RT).

25 g bottle.
