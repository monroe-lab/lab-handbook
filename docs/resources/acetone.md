# Acetone

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> (CH₃)₂C=O, the simplest ketone. Polar enough to mix with water in all proportions but also dissolves most nonpolar organics, making it a universal "bridge" solvent.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Pigment and chlorophyll extraction from plant tissue, glassware degreasing/drying, and a precipitation solvent for some proteins and polysaccharides.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Highly flammable (flash point −20°C); store away from ignition sources and use in a ventilated area.
