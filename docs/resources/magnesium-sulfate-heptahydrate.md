# Magnesium sulfate heptahydrate

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> MgSO₄·7H₂O ("Epsom salt"), highly water-soluble Mg²⁺ source; the seven waters give it a defined formula weight that makes weighing precise.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Mg²⁺ supplement in plant tissue culture media (a Murashige & Skoog component) and in bacterial growth media like LB and SOB to support cell wall integrity.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard under normal lab handling.
