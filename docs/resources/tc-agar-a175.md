# TC Agar A175

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Tissue-culture-grade [[agarose|agar]]: a purified linear polysaccharide from red seaweed that gels into a hydrogen-bonded mesh when boiled solutions cool below ~40°C.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> The solidifying agent in plant tissue culture media (Murashige & Skoog, callus induction, regeneration plates); "TC grade" means it's been cleaned of inhibitors and ions that would harm cultured cells.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Low hazard; molten agar will burn skin — handle with gloves.
