# Heat Block

Dry heating block with interchangeable inserts for different tube sizes (0.5 mL, 1.5 mL, 2.0 mL). Faster setup than a [[water-bath]] but slightly less uniform heat transfer. Used for quick incubations and enzyme denaturation.
