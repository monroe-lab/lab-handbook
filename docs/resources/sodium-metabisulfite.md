# Sodium metabisulfite

> ℹ️ **Chemistry**
> Na₂S₂O₅, which dissociates in water to bisulfite (HSO₃⁻); a reducing agent and antioxidant that releases SO₂ in acidic conditions.

> 💡 **Lab use**
> Antioxidant in plant extraction buffers to protect phenolics and proteins from oxidation; also a stable solid source of bisulfite for cytosine deamination chemistry.

> ⚠️ **Safety**
> Releases SO₂ on acidification — respiratory irritant; weigh in a hood and avoid mixing with acids in open air.
