What's an accession? The general concept of a thing — a particular tree, a genotype, a cell line, an MA line. Accessions have physical instances: 🌿 samples (tissue collections), 🧪 extractions, 📖 libraries, 🔀 pools — each with its own storage location. Track the concept here; link it from notebooks and protocols. For "where is this physically stored?" use the Lab Map.

New Accession

After creating, the accession opens in edit mode. Add physical instances from its Contents column — a sample (🌿 tissue collection), extraction (🧪), library (📖), or pool (🔀) with an of: link back to this accession. The editor lets you add more fields (sequencing type, detail sheet link, status note) as needed.
